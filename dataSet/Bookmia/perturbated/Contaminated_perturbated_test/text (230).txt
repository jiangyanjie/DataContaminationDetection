Mary was standing behind, while Elizabeth, Mary and Darcy were to entertain each other. Very little was said about the other two: Kitty was the only one in front of him ; Elizabeth was secretly unable to make her desperate resolution; and perhaps he could understand the same. They walked towards the Lucases, because Kitty wished she had been able to call upon Maria; and as Elizabeth saw no occasion for making it a general rule; Kitty left them she went boldly on with him alone. Let me thank you again and again for the name of all my family, for that generous compassion which I shall try to take so much pleasure from you. And, to bear so many mortifications, for the name of all my families, let me be grateful again. ``If you _will_ thank you_,'' Elizabeth replied, ``let me leave for yourself alone. If the wish of giving happiness to you might add force to the other inducements which led me on, I will attempt to deny. But your gratitude gives me nothing.
