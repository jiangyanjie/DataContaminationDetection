The children gathered for their next few days eagerly, and finally their calendar popped one off the calendar each night. The week was very long time . But Tuesday was not easy to deal with. But Tuesday did come. Dick and Julian, who shared a room, woke up at about the same moment, and stared out of the nearby window. "It's a lovely day, hurrah!" cried Julian, leaping out of bed. "I don't know why it should be very important that it should be sunny on the first day of April. Let's wake Anne." Anne slept in the next room. Julian got around the room - and shook her. "Wake ! Tuesday! The sun's shining." Anne woke up in a jump , pointing out joyfully. "It's come at last!" she said. "What's happening," said Julian, who thought it never would. Oh, it's an exciting feeling to wake up a little soon after , but today was a big day because it held them all very comfortably.
