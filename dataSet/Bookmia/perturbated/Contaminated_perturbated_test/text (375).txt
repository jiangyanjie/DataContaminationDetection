The word of pity came into Dorian's eyes, then Campbell ran the paper over his hand, took a piece of paper, and walked back through it. He pulled it over twice, folded it carefully, and pushed it across the table. Having gone he got up, and came to the window. Campbell looked at him in surprise, he came back to the window, pulled his hand up , and pulled back to the window. As he read the paper he looked at the paper. His face became ghastly pale, and he fell back in pain. A horrible sense of sickness came over him. He felt as if his heart was beating itself to death in some empty hollow. After two or three minutes of silence, Dorian turned round, and came and stood behind him, putting his hand above his shoulder. "I am sorry - Alan," he murmured, "but you leave me ."
