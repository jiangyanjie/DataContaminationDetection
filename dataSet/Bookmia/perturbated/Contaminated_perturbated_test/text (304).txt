Amy was in a fair way to the point, but her attitude for everyone petted her, but her small vanities and selfishnesses were handled nicely. One thing, however, rather quenched the vanities. She had to wear her cousin's skirt because Florence's face had a particle of taste, and Amy suffered deeply at having to wear a red instead of a blue bonnet, unbecoming gowns, and hats that did not exist.
