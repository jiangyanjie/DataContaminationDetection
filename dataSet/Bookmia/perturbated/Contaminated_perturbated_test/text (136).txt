His flesh was smacked into his right hand! shouted the prefect of studies. Stephen shook back his hand with a quivering flame and held out his left hand. His sleeve swished again as the spit was lifted and a loud crashing sound and a fierce maddening tingling burning saw his hand shrink together with the palms and fingers in the air. The flames burst forth from his eyes and, burning with shame and agony and fear, he took up his arm in terror and burst out into a whine of pain. His body shook with a palsy of fright and in shame and desperation.
