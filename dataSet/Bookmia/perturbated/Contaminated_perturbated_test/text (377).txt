In the light of the yellow light, I might have told Joe about the pale young gentleman, if I had not previously been betrayed into those enormous inventions to which I was trayed. Under the circumstances, I was convinced that Joe could hardly fail to discern in the pale young gentleman an appropriate passenger to bring me into the black velvet . I said nothing of him. Besides: that shrinking noise of Miss Havisham and Estella , but in the way that it had come upon me at the beginning, grew much more potent as time approaches. I had no complete idea what happened to me, but then, I told poor Biddy everything. Why it was important to me to do so, for Biddy , is that she had no concern in everything I told her, I did not know then, though I think I was a victim. Meanwhile, councils went on in the kitchen at home, fraught with a certain insupportable aggravation to the poor spirit. I, like Pumblechook, used to come over there at night for the first time discussing my prospects with my sister; and I did (and believe I did so) with less penitence than I might like to feel), and if these hands could have taken a linchpin out of the chaise-cart, they would have done it.
