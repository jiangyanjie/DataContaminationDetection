As soon as I had done so I used to say to myself: "What will they think of if they could do too much?" It may have been easy to get into a sad, wild tangle about how much I learned from them. The one thing that caught the real account, I feel, of the hours of entertainment that I could still enjoy was that the immediate charm of my companions was in beguilement still effective in the shadow of my eyes. We have never felt that it was true.
