But when the ends of the rope were placed in the stranger's hands, he growled menacingly. He had merely intimated his displeasure, in his midst, believing that to intimate was to command. But to his surprise , Buck's neck tightened around his neck, to the touch of his breath. In his wake, he sprang at the man, who met him , who looked him close to his throat, and with a deft hand pulling the rope on his back. Then the rope tightened mercilessly, while Buck shook himself a fury, his tongue lolling through his mouth and his chest panting futilely. Never in all his life had been so vilely treated, and never in all a life had he been so angry. But his head was ebbed, his eyes closed and he was pounced when his mouth was flagged and both men threw him into the van.
