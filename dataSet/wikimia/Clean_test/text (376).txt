On April 6, dozens of rockets were fired from Lebanon into Israel, wounding 3 Israeli civilians. The IDF has said that it has intercepted 25 rockets fired from Lebanon. Warning sirens had sounded in the town of Shlomi and in Betzet in northern Israel. According to Israel, the rockets were fired by Palestinian factions Hamas and PIJ with Hezbollah's approval.On early morning April 7, the Israeli Air Force retaliated by striking targets in Tyre, Lebanon and in the Gaza Strip.In a written statement, the United Nations Interim Force in Lebanon (UNIFIL) described the situation as "extremely serious" and urged restraint.
