Berlin 2030 klimaneutral (English: Berlin 2030 climate-neutral) was a citizens' initiative launched in Berlin in 2018. It was the subject of a city-wide referendum scheduled for 26 March 2023, commonly called the Klima-Volksentscheid (English: climate referendum). Voters will be asked whether the Climate Protection and Energy Transition Act of 2016 should be amended. The primary goal of the referendum is to legislate a goal of carbon neutrality by the year 2030, rather than the existing goal of 2045. Since the referendum proposes specific amendments to the law, its result will be legally binding.
