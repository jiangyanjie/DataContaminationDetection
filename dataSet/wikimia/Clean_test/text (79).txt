The 2023 DFB-Pokal Final will decide the winner of the 2022–23 DFB-Pokal, the 80th season of the annual German football cup competition. The match will be played on 3 June 2023 at the Olympiastadion in Berlin.The winner will host the 2023 edition of the DFL-Supercup at the start of the following season, and will face the champion of the 2022–23 edition of the Bundesliga,
