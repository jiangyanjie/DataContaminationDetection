The 2023 Guatemala City mayoral election will be held on 25 June 2023, to elect the mayor of Guatemala City, Department of Guatemala and thirteen members of the Municipal Council. Incumbent Ricardo Quiñónez Lemus is eligible for another term. Quiñónez of the Unionist Party is running for re-election with the support of Valor.
