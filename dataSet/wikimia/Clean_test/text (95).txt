On 26 February 2023, a boat carrying migrants sank amidst harsh weather conditions while trying to land on the coast of Steccato di Cutro, a seaside resort village near the town of Crotone, in the region of Calabria in southern Italy. The boat was carrying about 200 migrants when it sank, of whom at least 89 died, including at least 24 children. Eighty-one people
