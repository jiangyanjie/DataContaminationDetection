Miss Philippines Earth 2023 was the 23rd edition of the Miss Philippines Earth pageant, it was held at the Toledo City Sports Center in Toledo, Cebu, Philippines on April 29, 2023.At the end of the event, Jenny Ramp of Santa Ignacia, Tarlac crowned Yllana Marie Aduana of Siniloan, Laguna.
