Zerobaseone (Korean: 제로베이스원; RR: Jerobeiseuwon, stylized in all caps; abbreviated as ZB1) is a South Korean boy group formed through Mnet's reality competition Boys Planet in 2023. It is managed by Wake One Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Kim Ji-woong, Zhang Hao, Sung Han-bin, Seok Matthew, Kim Tae-rae, Ricky, Kim Gyu-vin, Park Gun-wook and Han Yu-jin.
