The 2023 Metro FM Music Awards is the 17th annual ceremony of the Metro FM Music Awards, the ceremony is sponsored by Metro FM and is set to premiere live on SABC1 on 6 May 2023 at Mbombela Stadium in Mbombela, Mpumalanga, South Africa.Rapper K.O leads the nomination list with seven nominations followed by rappers Sjava and the late AKA with six nominations each.
