Berlin 2030 klimaneutral (English: Berlin 2030 climate-neutral) was a citizens' initiative launched in Berlin in 2018. It was the subject of a city-wide referendum scheduled for 26 March 2023, commonly called the Klima-Volksentscheid (English: climate referendum).
