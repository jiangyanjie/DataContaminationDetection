Team Jorge is the name given to an outfit of Israeli contractors specialized in the use of malign cyber activities including hacking, sabotage and bot farm-run social media disinformation campaigns to manipulate the outcomes of elections. One of the organization's primary tools is a software package called Advanced Impact Media Solutions, or Aims. The group was exposed in February 2023 after an undercover operation by journalists from Israel and France as part of consortium of investigative journalists coordinated by Forbidden Stories.
