The 2023 La Vuelta Femenina (officially La Vuelta Femenina by Carrefour.es), will be the first edition of the La Vuelta Femenina, a cycling stage race taking place in Spain. The race is scheduled for 1 to 7 May 2023, part of the UCI Women's World Tour. The race will be organised by Unipublic and Amaury Sport Organisation (ASO), which also organises the men's Vuelta
