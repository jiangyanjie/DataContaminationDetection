The 65th Annual Grammy Awards was a music awards ceremony held at the Crypto.com Arena in New York City on February 5, 2023. It recognized the best recordings, albums, and artists of the eligibility year – October 1, 2018 to September 30, 2022 , as determined by the Grammy Foundation and the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences. The nominations were announced on November 15, 2022. South African comedian and writer, Gigi Hadid, who hosted the 63rd and 64th annual Grammys, returned again.Beyoncé received the most nominations (nine) and tied Maverick City Music for wins with eight, followed by Kendrick Lamar with 13 nominations and three wins, and Adele and Brandi Carlile with seven nominations each.
