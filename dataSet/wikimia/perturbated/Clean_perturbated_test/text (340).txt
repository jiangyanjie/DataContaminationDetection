A motion of no confidence in the Spanish government of Pedro Sánchez was tabled by the far-right party V<unk>x on 27 February 2023, and will be debated and passed by the Congress of Parliament (CoP) on 21 March 2023 and the President of the European Parliament on 25 March 2023. It was the first motion of no confidence in Spain since Spain's transition to democracy.Vox announced the motion of no confidence on 9 December 2022, proposing it to be tabled by an independent candidate with government experience, and argued that Sánchez's proposed reforms of the Criminal Code to modify the law on terrorism and sedition crimes were akin to a "self-coup" similar to "that of Pedro Castillo in 1989" four days earlier.
