Qatar Horticultural Exposition or Expo 2023 Doha is an International Horticultural Event that will be hosted by the Qatar Government. The Horticultural Expo 2023 Doha will begin on October 2, 2023 and end on March 28, 2024. Originally scheduled to be held from 14 October 2021 to 17 March 2022, but it was rescheduled to 2023 as a result of the COVID-19 21.The Bureau International des Expositions
