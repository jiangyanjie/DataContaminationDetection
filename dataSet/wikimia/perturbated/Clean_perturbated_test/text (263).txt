Brit Awards 2023, the 2023 edition of the award, organised by the British Recording Industry (BPI), was held on Thursday 11 February 2023 to recognise the best in British and international music. The ceremony took place at The O2 Arena in London, and was hosted by British comedian Mo Gilligan, who returns after hosting the 2015 edition.
