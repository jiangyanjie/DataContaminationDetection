The 2023 Pan American Games (Spanish: Juegos Panamericanos de 2023), officially known as the 2023 Pan American Games and commonly known as Santiago 2023, is a upcoming international sporting event organized and governed by the Pan American Games Organising Committee (PAGOC) to be held in Santiago de Chile from 20 October to 5 December 2023.
