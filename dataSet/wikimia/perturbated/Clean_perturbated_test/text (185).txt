From February 21 to February 24, 2023, a Chinese-operated high-altitude balloon operated in North American airspace, including North America, western Canada, and the contiguous United States. On February 4, the U.S. Air Force shot down the balloon over U.S. territorial waters off the coast of South Carolina by order of U.S. President Joe Biden. It was shot down by a U.S. F-22 Raptor.
