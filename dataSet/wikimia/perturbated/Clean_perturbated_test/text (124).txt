Cold Water Flat line is an American band formed in 1990 by Paul Janovitz (vocals and guitar), Ted Silva (bass) and Chris Williams (drums) when the trio studied at University of New Hampshire. Paul Janovitz is the brother of Bill Janovitz, lead singer and songwriter of Buffalo Tom.
