Intel Blockscale was a series of crypto-mining hardware, sold by the U.S. chip manufacturer Intel. The Blockscale product debuted in June 2022, and the entire product range was discontinued by Intel in April 2023. Intel has indicated that it will continue to supply Blockscale products to its existing customers until April 2024.The Blockscale chips were SHA-256 hardware accelerators designed for proof-of-work calculations.
