The 95th Academy Awards was a ceremony held by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS) on March 4, 2019 at the Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles, for films released in 2022.The event was broadcast in the U.S. by ABC Television and was executive produced by Ricky Kirshner and Glenn Weiss. Weiss was the director. Comedian and late-night talk show host Jimmy Fallon hosted the show for the first time after emceeing the 89th and 90th editions of the ceremony in 2017 and 2018.The Everywhere All owed led the ceremony with eleven nominations and seven wins, including Best Picture, Best Director , Best Original Screenplay , Best Screenplay for James Cameron and Daniel Scheinert, and three of the four major categories.
