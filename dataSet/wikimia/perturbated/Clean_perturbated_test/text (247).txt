2023 International Conference on Climate Resilient Pakistan, more commonly referred to as the 2023 Geneva conference, was the first-ever UN conference on aid to Pakistani Government after the devastating floods in 2022. The conference was organized by the Ministry of Climate and Energy and the United Nations, held on 9 January 2023 in Geneva, Switzerland.
