The Biodiversity Over the High Seas is a permanent instrument of the United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea . It was concluded by an intergovernmental conference at the United Nations Conference on the Law of the Sea on 4 March 2023. Its official title is Agreement under the United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea on the protection, conservation and sustainable use of marine biological resources and marine protected areas beyond national jurisdiction and is also known as the Biodiversity Beyond National Jurisdiction treaty.It has four main objectives: The establishment of new protected areas for marine biodiversity. The regulation of the sustainable use of marine biological resources, which can be used for the development of pharmaceuticals, in order to equitably share their benefits. The equitable access to research conducted in these areas. The setting of global standards
