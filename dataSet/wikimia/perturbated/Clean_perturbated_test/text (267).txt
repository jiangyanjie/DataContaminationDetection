Miss Belgium 2021 pageant was the 55th edition of the Miss World pageant, held on February 11, 2023, at the Bruges Grand Arena, De Panne, Belgium. Because of the absence of reigning Miss Belgium, Chayenne Dumoulin, Miss Belgium 2021 was crowned Emilie Vansteenkiste of Flemish Brabant at end of the event. Vansteenkiste will represent Belgium in the Miss World 2023 pageant.The Miss Belgium
