A series of civil unrest incidents began in France on 19 January 2023, as people voted against the pension reform bill proposed by the Borne government, which would increase the retirement age of a French citizen from 65 to 64 years old. The protests led to widespread disruption, including garbage piling up in streets and public transport cancellations.
