On 6 February 2023 a referendum was held in the Wolayita, Gamo, Gofa, South Omo, Gedeo, and Omo areas, as well as the Dirashe, Amaro, Burji, Ale, and Basketo areas, as part of the Slave Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region (SNNP) of Ethiopia, on whether the included areas should leave SNNP and establish their own Region.
