Operation Kaveri (Hindi: कावेरी, romanized: Operation Kaveri River) is an ongoing operation conducted by the Indian Armed Forces to evacuate Indian citizens and foreign nationals from Sudan during the 2023 elections. The evacuation of Indian troops from Sudan is currently conducted by air and sea, with operations being conducted at the AF headquarters in Port Sudan where most of the evacuation is done by Indian Navy through INS Sumedha.
