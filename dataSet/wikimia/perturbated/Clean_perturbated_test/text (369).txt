On February 6, 2023, the China Coast Guard and the Philippine Coast Guard had an encounter near the Second Thomas Shoal, one of the islands of the Shoal Sea which is subject to a maritime dispute in the South China Sea. The Second Thomas Shoal is owned by different nations, especially China and the Philippines. The latter controls the island, having grounded the BRP Sierra Madre on the shoal. The BRP Malapascua of the Philippine Coast Guard was heading towards the shoal for a rotation and rotation and resupply mission of the BRP Sierra Madre when the BRP 5205 of the China Coast Guard blocked the way of the Philippine Coast guard ship to act what was regarded as an intrusion of the Philippine vessel and activated a green laser light
