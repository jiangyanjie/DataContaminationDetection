The 11th annual Nickelodeon Kids Awards ceremony was held on March 4, 2023, at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles, California with Nate Burleson and Charli D'Amelio serving as hosts. It aired live on Nickelodeon's broadcast network and as a domestic simulcast with several other Paramount Global cable networks, and was broadcast or tape delayed across all of Nickelodeon's platforms.
