SpaceX Crew-7 is planned to be the seventh crewed operational NASA and ESA flight of the Dragon spacecraft, and the eleventh overall crewed orbital flight. The mission is scheduled for launch on 17 August 2023. The Crew-7 mission would transport four crew aboard the International Space Station . SpaceX will deliver, by 15 August 2022, one NASA astronaut, Jasmin Evans, one ESA astronaut, Andreas Mogensen
