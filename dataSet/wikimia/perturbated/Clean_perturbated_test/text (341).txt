The 2023 Summer Metro Manila Film Festival is the next iteration of the upcoming 2022 Summer Metro Manila Film Festival held in Manila and throughout the Philippines. It is to be organized by the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA) in collaboration with the Cinema Exhibitors Association of the Philippines. The theme of the film festival would be "Tuloy-tuloy ang Saya" (lit. 'The fun continues').The inaugural edition was supposed to be held in 2020, but was postponed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The 2022 edition is now considered to be the first iteration of the festival. Following the organization of the 2022 Summer Metro Manila Film Festival, the MMDA announced on March 12, 2023, that the upcoming 2023 Summer Metro Manila Film festival will be held from April 8 to 18, 2023.
