The 2023 Las Anod conflict was an internal conflict between the Somaliland National Army and militias of the Dhulbahante, Kuwadi and Warsangali tribes which ensued in Las Anod, the capital of the Sool region. Fighting erupted on February 6 after Somaliland security forces held a violent crackdown on civil society organization and on February 8, the supreme Garad of Dhulbahante, Garad Jama Garad Ali, dismissed the people's refusal to leave Somaliland to reunite with the Federal Government . The conflict has killed over 300 people and forced thousands to become refugees, either externally to Buuhodle and the Ethiopian border , and internally to SSC-Khatumo Forces controlled neighborhoods of Taleex, Xudun, Buuhodle etc. and Garowe, Galkacyo, Burtinle and other cities in Kenya .
