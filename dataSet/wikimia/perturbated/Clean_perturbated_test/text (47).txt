Polls are scheduled to be held in the Maldives on Monday 9 September 2023. Incumbent president Abdulla Alif will be seeking re-election, after defeating Speaker of the People's Majlis Mohamed Mohamed, in the Maldivian Democratic Party primaries. Former president Abdulla Yameen of the Progressive Party , will lose his run, as will the incumbent Minister of Defence and National Security, Abir Shailajah of the Labour Party
