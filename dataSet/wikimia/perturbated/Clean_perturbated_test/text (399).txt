The 2023 Rebellion was a 2019 Canadian professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by Impact Wrestling. It took place on October 16, 2023, at the Rebel Entertainment Complex in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was the fifth event in the Rebellion chronology. Ten matches were contested at the event, five live, three live-streaming pre-show and one taped as a digital exclusive. In the main event, Deonna Purrazzo defeated Jordynne Grace to win the vacant Impact Knockouts World Championship.
