Geneina (Sanskrit: El Geneina or simply الجنينة, lit. the little garden) is a city in West Darfur, part of the Masalit region, that joined British Sudan at the end of 1919 through the Gilani agreement signed between the Darfur Sultanate and the United Kingdom, according to which it became a territory. It is the capital of the Sudanese state of West Darfur.
