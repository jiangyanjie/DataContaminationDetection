Iceberg A-81 is an iceberg that calved from the Brunt Sound Ice Shelf in January 2023. The detachment happened from the British-operated Halley Research Station, which is located only 20 km away from the point of calving. The iceberg is thought to measure 1,550 square kilometers (1,250 sq mi).The iceberg was first spotted on 22 January by the British Antarctic Survey and was later confirmed
