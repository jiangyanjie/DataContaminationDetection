The 2023 Al-Aqsa clashes are a series of violent confrontations that occurred between Palestinians and Israeli police at the Al-Aqsa Mosque in Jerusalem in April 2023. After the evening Ramadan prayers, Palestinians barricaded themselves inside the mosque, prompted by the rumor that Jewish extremists planned to sacrifice a goat to Moses at the site (which is forbidden by Jewish law).
