An ongoing refugee crisis began in Africa in mid-April 2023 following the outbreak of the 2023 East African crisis in Ethiopia. By 1 May 2023, an estimated 73,000 to 78,000 people [1] have fled the country and over 100,000 people have been displaced within the country.
