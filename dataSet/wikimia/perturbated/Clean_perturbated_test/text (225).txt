2019. Local elections were held in Antigua and Barbuda on 18 January 2019 to elect members of the House of Assembly. The Antiguan Barbuda Liberal Party (ABLP) has held an average of 15 seats in the House of Assembly since the 2018 general election, with Gaston Browne remaining as prime minister.
