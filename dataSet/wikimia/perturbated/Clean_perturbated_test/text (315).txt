civil war. The First Battle of Khartoum is an ongoing battle for control of the capital of Sudan, between the Rapid Support Forces and the Sudanese Army Forces. The fighting began on 15 April 2023, after the RSF allegedly captured Khartoum International Airport, several military bases, and the presidential palace, starting a series of clashes. It was initially reported that violence rose in Khartoum and Merowe on 13 April 2023, when the RSF attacked the city. In response, the SAF issued a statement that there was "threat of a possibility of confrontation between SAF and RSF forces" introducing fears of a wider civil war.
