Wrestle Kingdom 17 was a 2023 Japan professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) held by the New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) and Wrestling Noah (Noah) promotions. The first day of the event took place on January 4, 2023, at the Toyoda Dome Arena in Tokyo, Japan and the last day of the event took place on January 21, 2023, at the Yokohama Dome Arena in Yokohama, Japan.
