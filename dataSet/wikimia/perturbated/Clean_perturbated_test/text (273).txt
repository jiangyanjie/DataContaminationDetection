The Asian Women's Club Volleyball Championship is the 23rd edition of the Asian Women's Club Volleyball Championship (AWCVC), an annual international women's club tournament organized by Asian Volleyball Confederation (AVC) with Volleyball Federation of Vietnam (VFRV) and FIVB. The tournament is currently held in Vĩnh Phúc, Vietnam from 25 April to 2 May 2023. The winner of the tournament qualified to 2023 FIVB Volleyball
