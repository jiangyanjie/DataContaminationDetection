Berlin 2030 klimaneutral (English: Berlin 2030 climate-neutral) was a citizens' initiative launched in Berlin in November 2018. It was the precursor to a city-wide referendum scheduled for 26 April 2019, commonly called the Berlin climate referendum (or climate referendum). Voters will be asked whether the Climate Protection and Energy Transition Act (KPLT) should be amended. The primary objective of the referendum is to set a goal for climate neutrality by the year 2030, rather than the existing goal of 2045. Since the referendum ask s for specific amendments to the law, its result will be legally binding. In order to pass, the referendum must be approved by a majority of voters on the day of the vote, in the form of approval from a quorum of over 25% of registered voters.
