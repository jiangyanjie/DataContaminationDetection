The sixth annual Hollywood Critics Association Creative Arts Awards, presented by Hollywood Critics Association will take place on February 24, 2019.The winners were announced on the official Facebook Page, Instagram and Twitter, while the 6th Hollywood Critics Association Film Awards will take place on the same day at the Beverly Wilshire Hotel, Los Angeles. The nominations were announced on the Hollywood Critics Association YouTube channel and app on December 29, 2022.Everything Everywhere and Once led the nominations with six, followed by The Batman and Top Gun: Maverick with five each. The former and Babylon won the overall CG awards with two wins each.A shortlist committee, led by Erik Anderson, AwardsWatch contributor and HCA member, was set up in December tasked with generating the shortlists for the HCA Creative Arts Awards.
