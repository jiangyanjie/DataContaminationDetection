NCT DoJaeJung (Korean: 엔시티 도재정; RR: Ensiti Dojaejeong; stylized as all caps) is the fifth sub-unit of the South Korean music band NCT, being managed by Yoonbi. The group consists of three members: Doyoung, Jaehyun, and Jungwoo. They gained international attention on 25 November 2009 with their extended play NCT Superstar.
