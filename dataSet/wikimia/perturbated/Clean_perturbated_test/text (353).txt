Puteri Indonesia 2023, (Lampungnese: , Javanese: ꦦꦸꦠꦺꦫꦶ ꦆꦤ꧀ꦢꦺꦴꦤꦺꦱ ꧇꧒꧐꧒꧓꧇) the 26th Puteri Indonesia pageant, will be held on 19 May 2023 in Jakarta, Indonesia. Puteri Indonesia 2022, Nicole De-Neefe Suardana of Jakarta SCR 5, will crown her successor at the end of the event. In this edition, Indonesia has lost the rights to select and send the representative to the Miss Universe competition. Puteri Indonesia 2022 will represent Indonesia at Miss International 2023. Puteri Indonesia Lingkungan 2022, Kimberly H. Goh of Jakarta SCR 8 and Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata 2022, Cindy May McGuire of Jakarta SCR 5 and Adinda Cresheilla of Java will crown their successors. Puteri Indonesia Pariwisata 2023 will represent Indonesia at Miss Supranational 2023 and Puteri Indonesia Lingkungan 2023 will represent Indonesia at Miss Charm 2024.
