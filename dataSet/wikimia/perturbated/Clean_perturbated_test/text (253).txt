The 2023 Metro FM Music Awards is the upcoming edition of the Metro FM Music Awards, the ceremony is sponsored by Metro FM and is set to premiere live on SABC1 on 6 May 2023 at Mbombela Stadium in Mbombela, Mpumalanga, South Africa.Rapper K.O leads the nomination s table with eight nominations followed by rappers K.I.T. and the late AKA with six nominations each.
