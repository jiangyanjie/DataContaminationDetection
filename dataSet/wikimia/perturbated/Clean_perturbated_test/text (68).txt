The 35th Lo Nuestro Latin Awards will be held at the Miami Dade Arena in Miami on February 23, 2023, with the most popular category being Latin Songs of 2022. The ceremony will be broadcast on Univisión and will be hosted by television presenters Alejandra and Adrián Uribe and radio presenters Alberto Sanchez and Paulina Rubio.The nominees were announced on January 23, 2023, with Colombian singer Sebastián Yatra
