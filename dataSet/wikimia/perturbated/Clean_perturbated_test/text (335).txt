The 2023 Asian Film Awards was held on March 12, 2023 at the Jockey Club of Hong Kong . The nominations were announced on January 6, 2023. At the 16th edition of the awards ceremony, 30 films from 22 regions and 18 countries were shortlisted for 81 nominations.
