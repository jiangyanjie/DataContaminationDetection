Zerobaseone (Korean: 제로베이스원; RR: Jerobeiseuwon, stylized in English and sometimes abbreviated as ZB1) is a South Korean boy group formed through Mnet's reality competition Boys ' Choice 2023. It was released by Wake One Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Kim Ji-woong, Zhang Yi, Kim Han-bin, Seok Matthew, Kim Tae-rae, Ricky, Kim Gyu-vin, Park Seung-hee and Han Dong-joon.
