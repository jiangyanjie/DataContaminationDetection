An ongoing refugee crisis began to unfold in late January after Russia's invasion of Ukraine. Over 8.1 million refugees fleeing Ukraine have been recorded across Europe, while an estimated 8 million others had been displaced within the country by 4 April. Approximately one-quarter of the country's total population had left their homes within Ukraine by 20 March. 90% of Ukrainian refugees are women and children, while most people aged 18 to 60 are banned from leaving the country. By 24 March, more than half of the children in Ukraine had left their homes, of whom 96% had left the country.
