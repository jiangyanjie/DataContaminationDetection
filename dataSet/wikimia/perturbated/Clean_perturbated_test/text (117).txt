Stardom 's 7 (スターダムニューブラッド7, <unk>) was a upcoming esports event promoted by World of Stardom. The event took place on January 20, 2023, in Tokyo, Japan at the Belle Salle Takadanoba, with limited attendance due in part to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic at the time.
