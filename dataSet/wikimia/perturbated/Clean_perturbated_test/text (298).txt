The 2018 57th Annual Teen Choice Awards were presented on January 15, 2023, at the Fairmont Century Plaza Hotel in Los Angeles, California, honoring the finest achievements of filmmaking and television programming . The ceremony was broadcast on The CW and hosted by Chelsea Handler, taking over the role from Robin Williams, who had hosted the show consecutively in the previous two years. As in the previous year, the film and television nominations were announced separately.
