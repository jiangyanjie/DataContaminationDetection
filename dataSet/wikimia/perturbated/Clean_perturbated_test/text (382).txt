Team Jorge is the name given to an outfit of Israeli s, primarily involved in the orchestration of malign cyber activities including election sabotage and bot nets used in social media disinformation campaigns to influence the outcomes of elections. One of the organization's primary tools is a software package called Advanced Impact Media Solutions, or Aims. The organization was exposed in February 2023 after an undercover operation conducted by journalists from Israel and France as part of a team of investigative journalists coordinated by Forbidden Stories.
