Global news flow (also referred to as international news flow) is a field of journalism that deals with the news coverage of different foreign countries. It describes and explains the flow of information from one country to another. Articles on global news flow often attempt to explain why certain countries are more prominent than others.. Along the years it has been discovered that the geographical location of countries plays a particularly crucial role in news prominence as well as on the coverage of international news agencies.
