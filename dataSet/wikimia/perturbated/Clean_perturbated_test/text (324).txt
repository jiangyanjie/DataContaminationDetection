The 2023 Night of Champions is the upcoming 10th Night of Champions professional wrestling and livestreaming event produced by the main roster of WWE. It will be held for wrestlers from the promotion's Raw and SmackDown brand divisions. The event is scheduled to be held on Saturday, July 13, 2023, at the Jeddah Super Dome in Saudi Arabia. This marks the first Night of Champions event to be held on a Saturday and the fourth night that WWE will be hosted in Saudi Arabia, subsequently being the third night that WWE will hold in the country as part of its 10-year partnership in support of Saudi Vision 2030.
