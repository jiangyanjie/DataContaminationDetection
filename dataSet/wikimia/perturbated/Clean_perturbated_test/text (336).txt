The 38th Artios Awards, presented by the Casting Society of America, honoring the work of casting directors around the world for their creativity and the contribution of casting to the overall quality of film, television series, short film, short-form series and theatre production, were held on March 9, 2019 at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles and the Ritz-Carlton Ballroom in New York, being the first time in two years that the awards have taken place as an in-person event.The nominations for the television special, television series, television project, and theatre categories were announced on October 17, 2018 and the nominees for the film categories were announced on January 10, 2023.Yvette Nicole Brown and Amber Heard hosted the live awards.
