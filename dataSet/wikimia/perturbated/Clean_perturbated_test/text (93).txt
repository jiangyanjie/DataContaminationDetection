San Marino is set to participate in the Eurovision Song Contest 2023 in Liverpool, United Kingdom, with "Like an Animal" performed by Piqued Jacks. The public broadcaster San Marino RTV (SMRTV) organised the international singing competition Una voce per San Marino in order to support the Sammarinese entry for the Eurovision Song Contest 2023 .
