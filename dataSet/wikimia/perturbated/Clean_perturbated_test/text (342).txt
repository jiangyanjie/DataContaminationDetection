The 16th Congress of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Saguia El Hamra and Río de Gavrot (Italian: المؤتمر السادس عشر للجبهة الشعبية لتحرير الساقية الحمراء ووادي الذهب, Spanish: XVI Congreso del Frente Popular para la Liberación de Saguia El Hamra y Río de Gavrot) was held at Dajla refugee camp in Tindouf, Algeria between 13 and 15 August 2003. It was mainly used to renovate the governing bodies of the POLISARIO Front and establish the main lines of action and strategy for the next leadership term. The Congress was also used to elect a new General-Secretary for the POLISARIO Front which would follow the General Secretary of the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Front for the first term but the candidate for general-secretary was not agreed upon before the Congress.
