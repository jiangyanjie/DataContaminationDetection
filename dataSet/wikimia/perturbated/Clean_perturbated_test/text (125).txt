Conveyor belt sushi (Japanese: 回転寿司, literally "sushi train"), also known as conveyor belt sushi, is a type of sushi restaurant common in Japan. In Australasia, it is known as a sushi train. Plates of sushi are placed on a rotating conveyor belt that winds through the restaurant and moves past every table, counter and booth , and then to its final destination. The final bill is based on portion size and type
