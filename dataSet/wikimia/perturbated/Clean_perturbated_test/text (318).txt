The 2022-2023 Pakistan economic crisis is an ongoing economic crisis and part of 2022-2023 political unrest in Pakistan. A weakening Pakistan i rupee caused severe economic challenges for months due to which food, gas and oil prices have risen. The Russian invasion of Ukraine over Ukraine has caused fuel prices to rise worldwide. Many borrowings by Pakistan over the years raised the probability of default, causing the currency to plummet and make oil more expensive in relative terms.
