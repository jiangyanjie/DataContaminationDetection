Dark Angel is a thrash metal band from Los Angeles, California, formed in 1981. The band's current lineup includes drummer Gene Hoglan, guitarists Eric Meyer and Gary Christine, frontman Ron Rinehart and bassist Jim Durkin. In its history, the band has gone through many lineup changes, including its current member Eric Meyer who has appeared on all four of its albums. Following the death of lead guitarist Jim Durkin in March 2023, there are nine members left on the current lineup of Dark Angel. Meyer, who joined in 1984, is the only member to appear on all four albums. Dark Angel's over-the-top style (extremely fast, heavy and lengthy songs with many tempo changes, and extended instrumental parts) earned them the nickname "The L.A. Caffeine Machine".
