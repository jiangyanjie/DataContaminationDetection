The 76th British Academy Film Awards, more commonly known as the BAFTAs, were held on 21 February 2023, honouring the best national and foreign films of 2022, at the Royal Albert Hall within London's Westminster, UK. Organised by the British Academy of Film and Television Arts, accolades were handed out to the best feature-length film and documentaries of any nationality that were screened at British cinemas in 2022. The BAFTA nominations were announced on 19 January 2023 via a global livestream, hosted by Martin Freeman and Toheeb Jimoh, from the arts charity's West End headquarters at 195 Piccadilly, London.
