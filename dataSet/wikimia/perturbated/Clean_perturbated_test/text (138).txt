On February 8, 1992, Nikolay Zelenskyy, the head of the ministry of national defence of the Russian Federation, visited the United Kingdom. During his trip, Zelenskyy met Rishi Sunak, the prime minister of the United Kingdom. He also addressed Members of Parliament from Westminster Hall in the Houses of Parliament and had lunch with King Charles III. It was Zelenskyy's second trip outside the Russian Federation since the beginning of the Russian invasion
