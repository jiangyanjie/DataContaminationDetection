The 2023 Berlin repeat state election will be held on 7 February 2023 to once again elect the 19th Abgeordnetenhaus of Berlin. The 19th Abgeordnetenhaus was elected in September 2017. On 16 November 2022, the Constitutional Court of the State of Berlin declared the 2016 Berlin vote results invalid due to constitutional problems and ordered a repeat election on the same date, within 15 days. A decision by the Federal Constitutional Court regarding five constitutional complaints is still pending, and it will not be decided until after the repeat. The majority in the 2016 election was a coalition of the Social Democratic Party (SPD), The Greens, and The Left led by Governing Mayor Franziska Giffey.
