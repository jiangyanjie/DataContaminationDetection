This article is about the 2023 flower protests in Russia. There have also been other flower protests, e.g. in Myanmar in 2016 on Suu Kyi's 76th birthday. The flower protests are a series of peaceful, silent protests which started in January 2023, against the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine. It is planned to take place in various cities.
