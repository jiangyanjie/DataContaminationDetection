Municipal election is set to be held in all eight constituencies on 31 October 2023. In accordance with changes made in 2018, election day for local councils, which will be a paid day , is set for 20 September 2024. The majority of councillors in municipalities, local councils and regional councils will be up for election on 8 October 2020, as will their leaders. Top-two runoffs will be held on 14 November 2020, for those localities where no leadership candidate won 40% or more of the vote in the general election. Voters in municipalities and local councils will pick their leader and their local committee using two separate ballots that are colored yellow and white respectively.
