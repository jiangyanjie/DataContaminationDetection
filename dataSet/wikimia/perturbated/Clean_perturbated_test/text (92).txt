The 2023 Coupe de France final was a UEFA European championship playoff match between Paris St-Germain FC and Toulouse to decide the winner of the 2022–23 Coupe de France, the 106th season of the Coupe de France. Toulouse FC won the match 5–1, claiming their third Coupe de France title.
