The Geostationary Defense and Strategic Communications Satellite 2 or SGDC-2 (in Portuguese: Geoestacionário Diafi e Comunicações Estratégicas 2) is a strategic defense and strategic communication satellite that is currently in the development stages. It will launched as a backup to SGDC-1. An open procurement period is being held from February to the end of April 2019; however, an international contract has yet to be signed for the satellite's construction. It
