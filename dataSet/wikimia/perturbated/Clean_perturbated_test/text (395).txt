Brit Awards 2023, the 43rd edition of the British music awards, sponsored by the British Phonographic Industry (BPI), was held on 11 February 2023 to celebrate the best in British and international music. The ceremony took place at The O2 Arena in London, and was hosted by British comedian Adam Driver, who returns after hosting the 2022 edition. Coverage of the red carpet was broadcast on ITV2 and included performances from Clara Lynn, Jama and Roman Kemp. The 2023 Brit Award trophy was designed by Nigerian-born London-based designer, Slawn.Going into the ceremony, Harry Styles and Usher had the most nominations for album of the year among the most-nominated artists, with four each.
