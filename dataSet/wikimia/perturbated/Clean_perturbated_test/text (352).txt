From January 28 to February 4, 2023, a satellite balloon was spotted in North American airspace, including Newfoundland, Labrador, Canada and the contiguous United States. On February 4, the U.S. Navy shot down the balloon over U.S. territorial waters off the coast of South Carolina, on the order of U.S. President Joe Biden. It was shot down by a U.S. Raptor. Debris from the wreckage was recovered and sent to the FBI Laboratory in Quantico, Virginia, for analysis.The American and Chinese militaries said that the balloon was for military purposes; the Chinese government maintained it was a civilian (mainly meteorological) airship that had been blown off course.
