Sumika (stylized as sumika) is an Japanese alternative rock band from Kawasaki, Kanagawa. The band debuted as an indie rock band in 2013, before being signed to a major label in 2018. The band has a concert theme known as Sumika [camp session] which is used throughout their performances.
