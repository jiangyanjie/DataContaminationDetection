The 2023 Rebellion was a professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by Impact Entertainment. It took place on April 16, 2023, at the Rebel Entertainment Centre in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was the fifth event under the Rebellion brand. Four matches were contested during the main event, including two on the pre-show and one as a digital exclusive. The main event featured Elias and Vince Purrazzo
