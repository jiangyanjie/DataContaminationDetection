The 2023 Las Anod conflict is an ongoing armed conflict between the Somaliland National Army and militias of the Majeerteen and Las Anod communities that ensued in Las Anod, the capital of the Sool State of Somalia. The conflict erupted in Las Anod on February 12, 2010, after Somaliland security forces held a violent crackdown on civil protests.
