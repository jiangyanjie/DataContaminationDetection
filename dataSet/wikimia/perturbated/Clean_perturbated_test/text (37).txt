The Circle Chart Music 2019 award ceremony was held at KSPO Dome in Seoul on February 18, 2023, to recognise the best artists , primarily based on Circle Chart Music's best songs of the year from December 1, 2021 to November 30, 2022. This award ceremony was hosted by Doyoung and MCs Alessandro and Rimmel. Doyoung was the most awarded artists, with 5 awards, followed by Blackpink with 4.
