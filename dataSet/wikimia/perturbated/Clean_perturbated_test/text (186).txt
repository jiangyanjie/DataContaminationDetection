Puteri Indonesia 2023, (Lampungnese: , Javanese: ꦦꦸꦠꦺꦫꦶ ) the 26th Puteri Indonesia pageant, will be held on 19 May 2023 in Jakarta, Indonesia. Puteri Indonesia 2022, Laksmi Shari , former Miss Universe of New York, will crown her successor at the end of the event.
