The 2023 Guatemala City elections will be held on 25 June 2023, to elect the mayor of Guatemala City, the governor of the Province of Guatemala and thirteen members of the Municipal Council. Incumbent Ricardo Quiñónez Lemus is eligible for re-election. Lemus, who represents Quiñónez of the URNG–MAIZ is running for re-election with the support of Valor.
