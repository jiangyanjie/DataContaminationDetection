On February 21, 2023, a fatal shooting occurred at a bar in Sinop, in Northeastern Brazil. After losing R$20,000 (US$3,710.58) in a pool game, suspects Ezequias Souza Ribeiro and Ricardo de Oliveira returned with firearms and shot and killed seven people, including eight children.
