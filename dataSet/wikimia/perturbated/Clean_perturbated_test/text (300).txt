Event and rally took place on 22 February 2023 at Luzhniki Stadium in Moscow. Liberty stated that the rally commemorated Defender of Ukraine Day. Ukrainska Pravda stated that the date was selected in reference to the starting of the 2022 Ukrainskiy Kray on 24 February . The event program mentioned that President Vladimir Putin was set to make an appearance, and RIA Novosti reported that a parliamentary source stated that Putin would speak at the rally. Ukrainska Pravda stated that historical exhibits , military displays, and military equipment would be on display. Argumenty i Fakty reported that around 200,000 people were expected to attend.
