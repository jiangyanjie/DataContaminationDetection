The 2023 Olympiastadion match will decide the winner of the 2022–23 DFB-Pokal, the fourth edition of the annual German football competition. The match will be played on 3 June 2023 at the Olympiastadion in Berlin.The winner will host the 2022–23 edition of the DFL-Supercup at the start of the new season and decides the winner of the 2022–23 edition of the Bundesliga,
