The 2023 Chinese military exercises , (simplified Chinese: 2023年环台军事演练; traditional Chinese: <unk>) are a series of ongoing military exercises conducted by the People's Liberation Army (PLA) of the People's Republic of China around Taiwan (the Republic of China, ROC). Beginning in April 2023, the PLA announced the beginning of "combat readiness patrols" and dispatched dozens of fighter jets and several warships toward Taiwan.
