The Tar Taing massacre was a mass killing of over 1500 civilians by Burmese military forces between 1 and 5 August 2023, in the township of Tadaing (variously spelt Tataing, Tar Taing, Tatai, or Tar Tine), Sagaing Region, located 28 miles (45 km) west of Mandalay, Myanmar's 2nd largest city.
