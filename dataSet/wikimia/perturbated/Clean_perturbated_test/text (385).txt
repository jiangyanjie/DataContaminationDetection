In journalism, local refers to coverage of local news, essentially a local version of the news, in a local context that would not have been in the interest of another locality, or would not have been possible or appropriate for news of national or international scope. Local news, in contrast to national or international , cater to the needs and interests of regional or local communities; they focus on local issues and events. The features of local news includes regional politics, weather, business, and environmental stories. Local news has been on the decline in recent years, according to a recent study. And as more and more Americans tap into streamed programming, local viewership is beginning to decline.
