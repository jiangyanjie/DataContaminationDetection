Triplemanía XXXI is an international Mexican professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event promoted and produced by the Mexican professional wrestling promotion Lucha Libre AAA Worldwide (AAA -W A). The event is being held on April 16, July 15, and August 12, 2023. The April 16 event took place at El Béisbol Monterrey in Monterrey, the July 15 event will take place in Los Angeles at the Estadio
