Severe Tropical Cyclone Michael, a severe tropical cyclone that washed up on the North Island of New Zealand and affected parts of Vanuatu and Australia in February 2023. Michael was the costliest tropical cyclone on record in the Southern Hemisphere, and its damages are estimated to be at least NZ$13.5 billion (US$8.4 billion), of which the provisional cost of insured damage is at NZ$2.5 billion
