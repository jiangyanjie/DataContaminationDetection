A social media newsroom is a company resource, set up to increase the functionality and usability of the traditional corporate newsroom. Social media newsrooms (SMNs) are intended to encourage dialogue and collaboration. Unlike traditional newsrooms, their content is accessible to more than journalists, but also to those with whom the company engages such as bloggers, their prospects, customers, and employees. Social media newsroom for businesses , customers or employees. It gives these stakeholders access to news, public relations announcements, images, videos and other multimedia files. In addition to posting press releases and news, companies can integrate social media like their blog and other social media sites such as YouTube, Flickr and Slideshow as well as their existing social media on their Facebook and Twitter accounts.
