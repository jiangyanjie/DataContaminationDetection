All -Star Festival 2023 was an interpromotional professional wrestling pay-per-view event organized by New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW). It was held on March 19, 2023 at the Hyogo Hotel in Bunkyo, Tokyo, Japan. The event, which was produced by NJPW wrestler Hiromu Takahashi, featured the top eight junior heavyweight wrestlers from over 12 professional wrestling promotions from around the world.
