The 6th Hollywood Critics Association Film Awards, presented by the Hollywood Critics Association, took place on the evening of January 24, 2023 at the Beverly Wilshire Hotel in Beverly Hills, while the winners of the 1st Hollywood Critics Association Creative Arts Awards were announced earlier in the day on social media platforms. The nominations for both events were announced on the HCA official YouTube channel and app on January 13 and 15, 2022, respectively; Jalyn Hall, Jude Law, and Madeleine McGraw announced the 2022 nominations. Comedian Tig Notaro also hosted the ceremony.Everything Everywhere All at once topped the nominations with eight, followed by The Banshees and The Fall with seven. The former also received twelve HCA Film Awards nominations, bringing the total to sixteen nominations. Everything Everywhere
