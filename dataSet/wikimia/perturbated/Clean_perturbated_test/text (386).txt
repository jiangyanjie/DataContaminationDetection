2023 International Conference on Climate Resilient Pakistan, more commonly referred to as 2023 Geneva conference, was a one-day conference on aid for Pakistan and the United Nations Government after the devastating floods of 2022. The conference was organized by the Pakistani Government and the United Nations on 9 January 2023 in Geneva, Switzerland. Shehbaz Sharif, the Prime Minister of Pakistan, and Antonio Guterres, the Secretary-General of the United Nations, addressed the one-day conference, which attracted more than 400 attendees including civil society, international financial organizations, and government representatives from around 40 countries. During the conference, Pakistan presented the “Resilient, Recovery, Rehabilitation, and Flood Risk Fund” (4RF) and seek international support and long-term partnerships for its implementation.
