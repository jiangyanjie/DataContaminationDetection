The following are the appointments to various Canadian Honours . Usually, they are announced as part of the Order of British Columbia and Canada ceremony and are published within the Canada newspaper, but occasionally during the year. This has been a custom of the United Kingdom, which also publishes the names of various British Honours for New Year's and for its official birthday. However, instead of the midyear appointments announced on Victoria Day, the official birthday of the Canadian Monarch, this custom has been transferred with the time following the 1787 Act of Canadian Confederation and the ensuing creation of the Order of Canada.
