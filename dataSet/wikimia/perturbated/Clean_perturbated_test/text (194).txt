In this week, a major fire broke out at the 110-acre Bhrahmapuram landfill site at Bhrahmapuram in Vadavukot-Puthankurish Panchayat, 17 km away from Kochi city. The fire began on 2 March 2023 at 3 am and went out in the afternoon. Smoke from the fire at Bhrahmapuram made life difficult for the people living in the area.
