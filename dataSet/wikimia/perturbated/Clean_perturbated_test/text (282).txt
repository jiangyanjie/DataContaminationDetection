The Group of New International Ministries (GNIM), commonly known as the Malindi cult and previously known as Servant P. N. Mackenzie Ministries, is an African religious movement based in Shakahola, Kilifi, Kenya. It was founded by Paul Nthenge Mackenzie and his two sons in 2003.
