Triplemanía XXXI is an annual professional wrestling pay-per-view event promoted and produced by the Mexican professional wrestling promotion , AAA Worldwide (AAA or Triple A). The event is being held on April 16, July 15, and August 12, 2023. The April 16 event will take place at Estadio de Béisbol Monterrey in Monterrey, Mexico, while the July 15 event will take place at Estadio Chevron in Tijuana and the August 12 event will take place at La Casa del Alamo de México in Mexico .
