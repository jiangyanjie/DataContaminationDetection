In April 2023, several sets of classified foreign intelligence documents of the United States government were shared on Twitter, Telegram, and Discord. Jack Teixeira, an airman first class of the Massachusetts National Guard, had allegedly stolen two sets of the documents at his parents' home in Concord, Massachusetts and posted them across the messaging app Telegram on a server named "Thug Shaker Central". The earliest posts dated to October 2022. The documents are primarily related to the Cold War, but also include foreign intelligence assessments of several countries including North Korea, South Korea, Russia, Iran, and the United Arab Emirates.
