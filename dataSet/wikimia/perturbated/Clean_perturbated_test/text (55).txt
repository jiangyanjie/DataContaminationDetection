Miss Philippines Earth 2023 was the 23rd edition of the Miss Philippines Earth pageant, it was held in the Toledo City Sports Center in Toledo, Cebu, Philippines on April 29, 2023.At the end of the event, the winner of Santa Ignacia, Tarlac defeated Anne Marie Aduana of Siniloan, Tarlac.
