On April 6, dozens of rockets were fired from Lebanon into Israel, wounding 3 Israeli civilians. The Israel Air Force said it has intercepted 25 rockets fired from Lebanon. Warning sirens had sounded in the southern city of Shlomi and in Betzet in northern Israel on April 5.According to Israel, the rockets were fired by factions led by the PIJ with Hezbollah's approval.On early morning April 6, the Israeli Air Force retaliated by striking targets in Lebanon and in the Gaza Strip.In a written statement, the United Nations Interim Force in Lebanon (UNIFIL) described the situation as "extremely serious" and urged restraint.
