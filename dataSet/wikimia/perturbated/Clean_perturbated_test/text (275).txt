The Anti-Homosexuality Bill, 2023 is a controversial bill that restricts freedom of speech on LGBT topics and criminalises homosexuality. On 21 March 2023, the bill was read a third time, and was then sent to President Museveni for assent. Museveni returned the bill to parliament for reconsideration on 21 July 2023.
