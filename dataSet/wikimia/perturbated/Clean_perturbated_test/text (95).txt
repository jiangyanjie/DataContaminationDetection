On 7 February 2023, a boat carrying migrants sank amidst harsh maritime storms while trying to cross the rocks of Steccato di Cutro, a seaside resort village near the town of Crotone, in the region of Calabria in southern Italy. The boat was carrying about 110 people when it sank, of whom at least 89 were migrants, including at least 24 children. Eighty-one people
