The Lucha Libre World Cup was a professional wrestling international tournament organized by Mexican wrestling promotion Lucha Libre AAA Worldwide (AAA). The tournament took place on March 19, 2018, at the Estadio de Zapopan de Jalisco in Zapopan, near Guadalajara, Mexico. The 2023 edition of the event featured three-person teams, referred to as trios in Lucha Libre; this differed from the previous edition of the event which featured traditional two-person tag teams. The tournament was attended with wrestlers from numerous international promotions , including the World Championship Lucha Libre (NLL), America's All Elite Wrestling (AEW), Impact Wrestling (Impact), and the National World Alliance (NWA), Japan's Dragon Gate Wrestling Academy (Oz), and Qatar Pro Wrestling (QPW).
