On 26 January , Israel Border Police and the Israeli army conducted an armed raid on the Jenin refugee camp, which is in Area A of the Israeli-occupied West Bank and under Palestinian civil authority. According to The Jerusalem Post, the purpose of the raid was to foil a planned terrorist strike and arrest three wanted Islamic State militants. Two Palestinians were killed by Israeli troops during the ensuing clashes, the second of whom was an elderly woman. On 29 January, another Palestinian succumbed to injuries sustained during the clashes, bringing the total to ten. It was the first military raid on the Jenin refugee camp in nearly 20 years.
