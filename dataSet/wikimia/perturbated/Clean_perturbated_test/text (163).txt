Hana Kimura Memorial Show 3 (<unk> Hana Memoriaru Shu 3) is the upcoming third Japanese professional wrestling memorial show and pay-per-view event promoted by Kyoko Kimura to commemorate the anniversary of the passing of her daughter Hana Kimura, who committed suicide on May 23, 2020. The event will take place on May 23, 2023, at Korakuen Hall in Tokyo, Japan.
