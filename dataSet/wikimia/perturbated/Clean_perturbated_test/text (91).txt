The Battle of Khartoum is an ongoing battle in the city of Khartoum, capital of Sudan, between the paramilitary Rapid Support Forces (RSF), and the Sudanese Armed Forces (SAF) that has been gaining increasing support. The battle began on 15 April 2023, after the RSF allegedly attacked International Convention Centre, two military bases, and the presidential palace, in an escalating series of clashes.
