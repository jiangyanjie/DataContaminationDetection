A series of reforms to reduce the judicial branch's influence over the balance of powers in Israel was proposed in January 2023. The reform has been spearheaded by Deputy Prime Minister , Minister of Justice , and the Chair of the Knesset's Constitution, Law and Justice Committee, Simcha Rothman. It seeks to curb the judiciary's influence over legal and public policy by revoking the Supreme Court's power to exercise judicial review, granting the government control over legislation and limiting the authority of legal advisors.If adopted, the reform would grant the Knesset the power to override judicial rulings that deem legislation passed by the Knesset as unconstitutional, by reintroducing the legislation and passing it with a majority of Knesset members.
