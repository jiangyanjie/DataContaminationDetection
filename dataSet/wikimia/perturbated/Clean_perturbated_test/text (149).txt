Femina Miss India 2023 was the second edition of the Miss India beauty pageant. After a two-year hiatus, the entire processes of competition from audition to the final s were revived and the concept took on-ground. On the finals held on 15 April, 2023 at Imphal, Manipur, each contestant from 29 states (including Delhi) was selected along with one collective representative for all Union Territories adding up to 30 participants
