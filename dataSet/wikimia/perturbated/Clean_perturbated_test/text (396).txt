Miss Belgium 2023 Pageant was the 55th edition of the Miss Belgium pageant, held on February 11, 2023, at the Plopsaland Complex, in De ignac. Because of the absence of reigning Miss Belgium, Chayenne Van Aarle, Miss Belgium 2023, Kedist Deltour crowned Emilie vansteenkiste, the Flemish Brabant at end of the event. Vansteenkiste will represent Belgium at the Miss World 2023 pageant.The Miss Belgium 2021 had to deal with misfortune starting the last hours before the event. Reigning Miss Belgium, Chayenne Van Aarle had a head injury in an accident a few hours before the event and wasn't allowed to take part in the event because she needed time to recover.
