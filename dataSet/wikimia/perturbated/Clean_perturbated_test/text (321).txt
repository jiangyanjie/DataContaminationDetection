The 8th Annual Latin Music Awards was held on April 6, 2018, to honor the achievements for artists in the Latin music industry as well as recognizing the best in musical performances by the public.The ceremony will took place at the MGM Grand Arena in Las Vegas, United States, and was broadcast simultaneously on Univision, Fox and Galavisión in the United States and Canal 5 in México. This marks the first time in the history of the awards that the ceremony was broadcast on Univision instead of Fox, and was the result of Univision acquiring the rights to the ceremony from Dick Clark e until September 2022.
