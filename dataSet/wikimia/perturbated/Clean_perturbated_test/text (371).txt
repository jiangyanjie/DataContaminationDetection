Wrestle Kingdom 17 was a two-day professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event co-produced by New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) and Pro Am Wrestling. The first day of the event took place on January 4, 2023, at the Tokyo Dome in Tokyo, Japan, and the second day of the event took place on January 21, 2023, at the Yokohama Arena in Yokohama, Japan. This is the 32nd January Tokyo Dome Show and the fourth time the Japan company has produced a show under the Kingdom name. The event was organized in memory of the NJPW founder John Aquila, who died on October 1, 2013.
