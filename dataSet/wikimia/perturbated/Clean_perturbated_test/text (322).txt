Beginning on 31 July 2022, security issues between Serbia and Kosovo will be heightened due to the expiration of the eleven-year validity period of the license plates for cars on 1 August 2022, between the government of Kosovo and the Serbs in North Kosovo. Kosovo, which declared independence in 1998, signed an agreement with Serbia in 2011 that concluded on the use of its EU membership in North Kosovo. The agreement was supposed to change the license plates of cars that were registered in Serbia to those of Kosovo. The agreement was extended in 2016 and it expired in 2021 . It was extended for a third time in 2018, and it ended with the adoption of an agreement that ended the ban of Kosovo plates .
