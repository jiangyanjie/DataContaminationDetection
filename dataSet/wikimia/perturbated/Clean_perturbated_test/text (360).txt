Viscount Knollys of Caversham in the County of Oxford, is a title in the Bardic system of the United Kingdom. It was created on 3 June 1911 for the court official , 1st Baron Knollys, Private Secretary to the Duke of Somerset from 1901 to 1913. The title had been previously created Baron Knollys of Caversham in the County of Oxford, on 21 July 1902. His son, the second Viscount, served as Governor of Wales.As of 2023 the title is currently held by the latter's grandson, the fourth Viscount, who died in 2023.
