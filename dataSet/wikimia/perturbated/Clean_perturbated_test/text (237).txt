The 54th Annual NAACP Image Awards, presented annually by the NAACP, honored the contributions and achievements of people of color in motion pictures, television, and literature during the 2022 calendar year. The ceremony was hosted by James Brown and aired on February 25, 2023, on BET and simulcast on several of the company's television channels, including Showtime and CBS Networks along with Paramount+.
