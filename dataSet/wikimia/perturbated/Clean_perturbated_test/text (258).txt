The 2023 Berlin City Council election was held on 12 February 2023 to again elect the 18th parliamentary district . The 19th Abgeordnetenhaus was originally elected on 26 September 2021. On 16 November 2022, the Constitutional Court of the State of Westphalian found the results invalid due to numerous irregularities and called for a repeat election within 90 days.
