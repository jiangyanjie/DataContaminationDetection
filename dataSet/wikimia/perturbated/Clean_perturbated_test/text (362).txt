The 20th Korean Music Awards was held in Seoul, South Korea on March 5, 2023. Hosted by the Korean Music Awards Selection Committee and sponsored by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism and the Korea Digital Content Agency, the ceremony recognized the best music released in South Korea between December 1, 2021 and November 30, 2022. Unlike other South Korean musical awards, the winners of the Korean Music Awards are selected based on achievement rather than votes. Winners are determined by the Korean Music Awards Selection Committee panel comprising producers, musicians, record producers, academics, and other music industry professionals.
