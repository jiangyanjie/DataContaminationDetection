The 75th Primetime Emmy Awards will recognize the best in American prime time television programming from September 18, 2022, until May 31, 2023, as announced by the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences. The ceremony will be held on September 18, 2023, and will be followed by the Golden Globes to be presented on May 31, 2023, and the American Arts Emmys to be presented on November 7, 2023 and
