The 29th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards, honoring the best achievements in film and television performances for the year 2022, were presented on February 28, 2023, at the Fairmont Century Hotel in Century City, California. The ceremony was streamed live on Netflix's YouTube channel, starting at 8:00 p.m. Eastern / 5:00 p.m. PST.
