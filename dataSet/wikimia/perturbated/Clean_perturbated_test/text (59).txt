In 2022, heavy fighting will break out in the Democratic Republic of the Congo between Rwandan and Congolese forces, which have caused several alleged attacks by Congolese and Rwandan forces on each other's territory. Rwandan forces have been caught in the DRC multiple times and fighting alongside Congolese rebels. The crisis is related to an ongoing offensive beginning in March 2021 by rebels in the country led by the Section 23 Movement
