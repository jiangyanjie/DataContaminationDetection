An online newsroom (also known as a pressroom, mediaroom, press center or newsroom) is a web page or site that contains distributable information about a corporation or its operations to the public. The online newsroom was initially created by corporate communicators and public relations firms to target traditional media outlets, fundamentally newspapers, magazines, radio stations and television stations. Multiple public relations interests are served by online newsrooms including media relations, investor and financial community relations, and consumer social media relations. Most online newsrooms provide press releases and kits, photographs, logos, audio, video, executive biographies, contact information, company news, awards and more.
