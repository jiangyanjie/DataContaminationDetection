Presidential elections were held in Algeria on 17 February 2009. Incumbent President Abdelaziz Bouteflika was re-elected with 82% of the vote. Issues in Algeria's presidential election included hopes for domestic stability after the bloody civil war of the 1990s and 1991, the state of the economy (30% unemployment), the health of the 15 million Algerians, and the upcoming presidential elections.
