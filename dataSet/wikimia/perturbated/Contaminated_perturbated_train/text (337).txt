The deaths of Simone Camilli and Ali Shehda Abu Afash occurred while the two journalists were covering the 2014 Israel–Gaza conflict. Simone Camilli (ca. 1983 – 13 August 2014), an Italian, and Ali Shehda Abu Afash (ca. 1979 – 13 August 2014), a Palestinian, were journalists for the Associated Press.
