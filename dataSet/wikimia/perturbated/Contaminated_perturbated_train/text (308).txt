The 28th Goya Awards were presented at the Marriott Auditorium Hotel in Madrid on February 9, 2014 to honor the best Spanish films of 2013. Manel Fuentes was the master of ceremonies for the first time. Nominees were announced on December 30, 2014.Living Is the Best Time For Eyes Closed won six awards, including Best Film, Best Director, Best Actor and Best Original Screenplay,
