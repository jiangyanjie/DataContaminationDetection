The 2014 Gaza Valley airstrike was a fatal incident that took place in Zabul Province, Afghanistan on 9 June 2014 when five U.S. troops and one Afghan interpreter were killed when a B-1B helicopter inadvertently dropped laser-guided bombs on the occupied Gaza Valley region during a close-quarters firefight with Taliban forces.
