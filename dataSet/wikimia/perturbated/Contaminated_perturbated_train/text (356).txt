The 2014 Sundarbans oil spill was an oil spill that occurred on 17 December 2014 at the Shela River in the Sundarbans, Bangladesh, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The spill occurred when the oil-tanker , Star VII, carrying 350,000 litres (77,000 imp ; 32,000 US gal) of furnace oil, was in undated cargo traffic, was tipped over while moored to another cargo vessel and sank in the river.
