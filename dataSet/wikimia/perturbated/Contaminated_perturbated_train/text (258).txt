The Channel O Music Video Awards took place on 8 September 2014 at the Cape Town City Centre in Soweto. Nominees were announced on 4 September. Cassper Nyovest, Diamond Platnumz and Davido led the nominations with 3 nominations each. DJ Khaled for his song "Pluto", which features the singing trio Beatenberg, followed with 4.
