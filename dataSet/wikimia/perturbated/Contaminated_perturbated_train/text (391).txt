The 2015 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships was the 2015 edition of Ice Hockey World Junior Championship, played from November 23, 2014 to January 13, 2015. It was co-hosted by Toronto, Ontario, and Montreal, Quebec, Canada, and organized by Hockey Canada, Hockey Quebec, the Ontario Hockey League, the Montreal Canadiens, Maple Leafs Sports and the NHL, among others.
