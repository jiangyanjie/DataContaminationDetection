Operation Okra is the Australian Defence Force (ADF) contribution to the campaign against the Islamic State of Iraq and Syria. The force is part of Joint Task Force 633 in the Middle East. The operation commenced on 31 August 2014 and its initial stated aim was to combat ISIL threats in Iraq, but by 30 September 2014 Australian airstrikes were extended to Syria.
