Global Wars was a professional wrestling event co-produced by the American Ring of Honor (ROH) and Japanese New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) promotions. The event took place on May 29, 2008 at the Scotiabank Arena in Toronto, Ontario, Canada and was aired live on the Internet Pay Per View (iPPV) through Ustream.
