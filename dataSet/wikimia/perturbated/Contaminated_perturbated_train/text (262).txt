2014 Kunar offensive was a 2014 armed conflict of the War in Afghanistan between the Afghan Army (Afghan Army) and Pakistan-based terrorist group, Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan (TTP). It occurred in the Dangam District of Kunar province, in Afghanistan bordering on Pakistan.The offensive was launched by the Afghan Army on 22 December 2014 after Kunar province had been the site of multiple armed clashes since 12 December
