Miss France 2015, the 85th edition of the Miss France pageant , was held on December 6, 2014 at Zénith d'Orléans in Orléans. Miss France 2014, Flora Coquerel of the Bourgogne district (Royaume de Loire) crowned by Camille Cerf a at the end of the pageant, represented France at Miss Universe 2014. It was the first time the pageant took place in Orléans
