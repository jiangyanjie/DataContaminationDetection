The Cemfjord was a Cyprus-registered cargo ship which foundered in the Pentland Firth off the coast of Scotland on 24 January 2015. Built as the shipping ship Margareta in 1984, she was registered as the Cemfjord to carry cement in 1998 and was en route from Aalborg, Denmark to Cheshire, United Kingdom when she capsized in bad weather and sank 88 metres (270
