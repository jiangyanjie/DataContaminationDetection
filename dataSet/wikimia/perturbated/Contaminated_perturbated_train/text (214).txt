Miss Brazil World. It is the 25th edition of the Miss Brazil World pageant and the fifth produced by MMB Productions & Events. The contest took place on August 9, 2010. A total of 467 candidates from the state of Rio de Janeiro, the Atlantic state, the Federal District and various Insular Islands competed for the title.
