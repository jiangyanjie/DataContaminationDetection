Around 5:30 AM on October 22, 2014, a passing motorist saw an Asian woman walking north along the Ingraham Trail , on the outskirts of Yellowknife in the Northwest Territories, Canada. Two days later, after reading in the local media that 45-year -old Yoshikubo (吉窪昌美) Yoshikubo, who had been visiting the city from her native southern Japan, had gone missing, she reported the sighting.
