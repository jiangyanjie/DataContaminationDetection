Wrestle Kingdom 8 in Tokyo Dome was a professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by the New Japan Pro-Wrestling League (NJPL), which took place at the Tokyo Dome in Tokyo, Japan on January 4, 2014. It was the last in the series. It preceded the 23rd January 4 Tokyo Dome Show and was held under the "Wrestle Kingdom" name. Like the previous year, it aired worldwide on internet pay-per-view
