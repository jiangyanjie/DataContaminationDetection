On the morning of 16 November 2014, two Palestinian men from Jerusalem entered Kehilat Bnei Torah synagogue, in the Har Nof neighborhood of Jerusalem, and attacked the praying congregants with axes, knives, and a gun. They killed four dual-nationality worshippers, five Druze priests and three police officers, and wounded a responding Druze police officer, who later died of his injuries.
