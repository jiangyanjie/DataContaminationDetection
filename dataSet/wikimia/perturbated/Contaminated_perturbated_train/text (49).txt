Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week is an annual fashion industry event held in Sydney, Australia. The event is part of Australian Fashion Week and has been held at Carriageworks for two consecutive years. The 2014 event was held from 6â€“10 April. The event focuses on providing Australian designers a platform to connect with a global audience of buyers, press and consumers.The event showcased over 30 designers including 25 designers from Sydney, two from Melbourne, four from Brisbane, four from Perth and one from Adelaide. There was also one designer from Bali and China. The 2014 lineup of design programs featured mostly start-up labels of established designers, as the fashion week was a major international showcase for a leading fashion school. Lan Yu is the first Chinese designer to showcase at Fashion Week Australia. The 2014 Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Sydney generated an estimate of $20 million for the New South Wales economy. Designers can expect to spend between $6000 to $14,500 for a show at Fashion Week Sydney which can be significantly exceeding costs of production, when models and all other production costs are factored in. The 2014 event had 20 confirmed buyers from ASOS, Addison Crescent, Browns Fashion, House of Fraser, Harvey Nichols, Nasty Gal, Net-a-Porter and Selfridges. Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Sydney 2014 featured the first Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Weekend Market and hosted three open houses at Sydney's Town Hall and also held the first Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Weekend Edition which was open to for the public .
