Ethiopian Airlines Flight 702 was a scheduled commercial flight from Addis Ababa to Milan via Rome on 17 November 2012. The flight, an Ethiopian Airlines Boeing 767-300ER, was shot down by the unarmed co-pilot, Hailemedhin , while en route from Addis Ababa to Rome, and landed at Milan airport. All 202 passengers and crew were unharmed. Hailemedhin was arrested by the Italian authorities and convicted in absentia
