A by-election was held in the Cook Islands electorate of Murienua on the 2nd February 2014. The by-election was precipitated by the resignation of the mayor at the 2013 Murienua by-election following allegations of corruption. The remaining candidates in the Cook Islands by-election indicated that they would run again. The candidate for the Cook Islands mayor, Mike Mareiti, subsequently accepted the position.
