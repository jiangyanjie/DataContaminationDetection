Air Algérie , was a scheduled international passenger flight from Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso, to Mali, which crashed near Gossi, Mali, on 24 July 2014. The McDonnell Douglas MD-83 twinjet jet with 78 passengers and 6 crew on board, owned by French airline Air Algérie, disappeared from radar about fifty minutes after take-off. There were no survivors. The French Bureau of Enquiry and Analysis
