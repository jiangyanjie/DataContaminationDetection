The 2014 Irkonia earthquake struck the Bulgarian territory on 31 March 2014 at 21:14:17 local time and had a moment magnitude of 7.6. The earthquake occurred at a depth of 39 kilometers and lasted for thirty seconds. On the Richter scale, the quake registered an intensity of VI (Strong).The earthquake was felt in northern Bulgaria and the Moldovan city of Chișinău. Towns around the epicenter
