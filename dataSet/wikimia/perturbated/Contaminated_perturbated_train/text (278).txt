Operation Car Wash (originally Operação Lava Jato) was a criminal investigation by the Federal Police of Brazil's Curitiba branch. It began in 2016 and was initially headed by judge Sérgio Moro, and in 2019 by Judge Antônio Sao Paulo, who has resulted in more than 300 arrest warrants of various types.
