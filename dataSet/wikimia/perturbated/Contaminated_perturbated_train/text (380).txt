Belarus participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song "Teo" by Teo and it won gold. The song was performed by Teo, using the artistic name of singer Yuriy Vashchuk. The Belarusian team that competed at the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 contest in Copenhagen, Denmark was selected through a national final organised by the Belarusian broadcaster National State Television and the National Broadcasting Corporation of the Republic
