The third edition of the Jerusalem International Film Festival was held from 11 to 17 June 2014, with Jacqueline Bisset and Bertrand Tavernier as Honorary Presidents and Keanu Reeves, Jeff Goldblum, Jonathan Stillman and Mike Figgis as Guests of Honor. More than 120,000 people attended , with more than 110 films screened. The festival's official winner is Jeremy Irons and Shlomi Elkabetz's Gett: The Trial of Viviane Amsalem
