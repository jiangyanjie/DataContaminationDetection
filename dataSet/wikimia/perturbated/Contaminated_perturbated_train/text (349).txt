The World Scrabble Championship 2014, renamed by MSI to Scrabble Champions Tournament in 2013, was held at the ExCeL London Exhibition and Convention Centre, in London, between 18-21 November 2014. It was the first time that the World Championship had become an open event , and MSI invited teams of ten teams to compete. However, due to the high number of entries and high venue costs, only 108 players entered
