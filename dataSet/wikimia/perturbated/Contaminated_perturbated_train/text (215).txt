The Pocklington Iron Age Cemetary is a prehistoric cemetery discovered in 2014 on the outskirts of Pocklington in the East Riding of Yorkshire, England. Excavations carried out on an ongoing basis since 2014 have uncovered more than 160 skeletons and more than 70 prehistoric human skulls that date to the Middle Ages .
