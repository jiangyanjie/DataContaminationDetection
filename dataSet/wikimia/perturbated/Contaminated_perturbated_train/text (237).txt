Blockupy is a German labour group and a group of politicians campaigning against austerity. The organisation includes Austria's Social Democrats and Germany's Linkspartei.Representing employees of supranational financial institutions such as the “troika”: European Commission (EC), European Central Bank (ECB) and International Monetary Fund (IMF), the name is derived from the Occupy movement , which split up in Wall Street in 2001.
