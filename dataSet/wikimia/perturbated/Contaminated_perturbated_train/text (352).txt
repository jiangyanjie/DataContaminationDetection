On 4 September 2014, 82-year-old Palmira Silva was beheaded in her back garden in Hackney, London, by 25-year-old Nicholas Salvador, who also went on a murder rampage. Friends of Salvador had recently noticed odd behaviour, including drug and alcohol abuse and an obsession with videos of beheadings. Psychiatrists found evidence that Salvador had been repressed. On 8 May 2015, he was found not guilty
