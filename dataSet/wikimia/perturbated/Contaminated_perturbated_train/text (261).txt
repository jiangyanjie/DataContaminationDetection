Late in the afternoon of December 11, 2014, China's internet suffered a major failure. The country's DNS infrastructure, which is responsible for translating domain names into IP addresses, started directing unrelated domains of various TLDs to the unresponsive IP address 65.49.2.178 on the night of November 12, 2014 (UTC+8).
