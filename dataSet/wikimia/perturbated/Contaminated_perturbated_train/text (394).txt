The 2014 DFB-Pokal final was a final between the holders and the winner of the 2014 DFB-Pokal, the 71st season of Germany's premier football competition. It was played on 17 May at the Olympiastadion in Berlin. In the final, Borussia Dortmund played Bayern Munich.
