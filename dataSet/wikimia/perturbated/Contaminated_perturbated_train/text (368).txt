The 2014 –15 Coupe de France Final was the crowning winner of the 2013–14 Coupe de France, the 97th season of France's premier football cup. It took place on 3 May at the Stade de France in Saint-Denis, Paris. In the final, Monaco defeated Toulouse 2–0 in a Derby Breton to win their second Coupe de France title.
