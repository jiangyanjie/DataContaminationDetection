Artush v. Karen is an international human rights case regarding the rights of Artush Petrosyan and his son Karen Petrosyan , a Russian national from Chinari , capital of the eastern province of Armenia, who died while in captivity in Azerbaijan in 2014. The judgment of the European Court of Human Rights on 8 November 2015 originated in an application (no. 32427/16) in the European Court by the Republic of Azerbaijan
