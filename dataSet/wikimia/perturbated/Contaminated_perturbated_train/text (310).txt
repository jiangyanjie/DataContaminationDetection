The Hotesur scandal is one of the most infamous cases in Argentina . Hotesur is a firm that administers the hotels in Calafate that belong to Cristina Fernández de Kirchner and her family. Initially, it was accused of not paying taxes, but the firm was later found out and eventually brought before a federal judge , whose decision led to suspicions about a possible scheme for money laundering, involving the businessman Lázaro Báez as well.
