Presidential elections were held in Afghanistan on 5 April 2014, with a second round on 14 June. Incumbent President Hamid Karzai was not able to run due to the corruption scandal. The registration period for presidential nominations was open from 16 September 2013 until 6 January 2014. A total of 27 candidates were confirmed to be running for president in 2013.
