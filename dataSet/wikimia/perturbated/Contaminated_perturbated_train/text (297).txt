Fantastica s , a series of five professional wrestling events co-produced by Japanese promotion New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) and Mexican promotion Champions Mexicanos de Lucha Libre (CMLL) taking place between January 15 and 19, 2014. This was the fourth year in which NJPW and CMLL had teamed up to hold Fantasticas Mania events, and the first time they held five events in one year,
