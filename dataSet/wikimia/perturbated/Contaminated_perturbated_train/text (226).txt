The 2014 Iquique earthquake struck off the northwest coast of Chile on 15 April, with an estimated magnitude of 8.2, at 20:46 CET (23:46 UTC). The epicenter of the earthquake was about 95 kilometres (59 mi) northwest of Iquique.
