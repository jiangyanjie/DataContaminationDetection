Election to the 6th convocation of the Moscow Duma took place on September 5, 2014. Voting took place on September 14, 2014. The elections were held in full proportional majority . 35 deputies were elected in 45 single-member constituencies (previously there were 35 deputies in the Moscow Duma) out of 258 candidates.
