The Clacton and Middleton by-election was held on 9 October 2014 in the Clacton and Middleton constituency following the death of its MP Jim Dobbin on 6 September 2014. It was held on the same day as the Clacton by-election. The Conservative Party narrowly held the constituency, and won with a recount.
