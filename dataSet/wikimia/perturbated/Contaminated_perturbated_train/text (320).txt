The Concept of Targeted Poverty Alleviation (simplified Chinese: 精准扶贫; traditional Chinese:精準扶貧, pinyin: jīng zhǔn fú pín) was first raised by Xi Jinping, General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party to combat poverty in China. Targeted poverty alleviation plays an important role in poverty alleviation strategy, as a tool to contribute to the Party's century goal of “comprehensively good society” and “moderately prosperous society” that is the first objective
