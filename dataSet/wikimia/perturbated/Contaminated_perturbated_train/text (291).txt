Miss Universe Thailand 2014, the 15th Miss Universe Thailand pageant was held at the Siam Hall, Siam Paragon in Bangkok, Thailand, on May 17, 2014. The contestants visited the floating island of Roi Et. before flying back to Bangkok for the final stage. Chalita Yaemwannang, Miss Universe Thailand 2013, declared her successor and crowned her successor at the end of this contest, in the final round, broadcast live on Channel 3. Weluree
