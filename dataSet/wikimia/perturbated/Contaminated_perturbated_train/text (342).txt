The Balkan Athletics Indoor Championships was the 19th edition of the annual indoor track and field competition for athletes from the Balkans, organised by the British Athletics Association (BAA). It was held on 22 February at the Ataköy Sports Complex in Istanbul, Turkey. Ivana Španović broke the Serbian record to win women's long jump with a mark of 6 m 11<unk>2 (22 ft 8+1⁄4 in).
