A general election was held on 20 November 2014 for the parliamentary constituency of Great Strood , South East England. The sitting Member of Parliament, Mark Reckless , won the election on joining the UK Independence Party (UKIP), from the last general election he resigned his seat.Reckless retained the seat, and polled 42.1% of the vote as the winner.
