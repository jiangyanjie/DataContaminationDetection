The World Cultural Council performed its 31st Award Ceremony on November 17, 2014 at the Dipoli Hall, Otakaari campus, Aalto University, Espoo, Finland. Aalto University is the host and co-organizer of the event, which was the final event prior to the Aalto University Academic Seminar on "The Impact of Universities on Global Competitiveness and Economic Growth."The Council presented 30 world cultural awards, honorary PhDs and honorary doctorates to distinguished scientists.
