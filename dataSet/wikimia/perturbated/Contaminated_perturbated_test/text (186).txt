Sociedad Polar Científica de Unión , Union Glacier Joint Antarctic Station) or Union Glacier Station is a summer Antarctic base of Chile, situated on Union Glacier in the Chilean Antarctic Province. It is jointly operated from November to January by the Chilean Antarctic Institute and the three armed forces of Chile. It was inaugurated on 4 January 2014 by Chilean President Sebastián Piñera . In June 2014 the Chilean Antarctic stations Teniente Santa Mar<unk>a and Teniente Antonio Huneeus were dismantled and their equipment moved to Union Glacier Station.
