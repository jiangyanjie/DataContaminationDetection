The Estación Polar Científica Conjunta Glaciar Unión (or the United States Antarctic Scientific Polar Station) or the Chilean Antarctic Joint Station is a summer Antarctic base of Chile, situated on the edge of the South Georgia Strait in the Ellsworth Mountains. It is jointly operated from November to January by the US Naval Expeditionary Force and by the armed forces of Chile. It was inaugurated on 4 January 2014 by Chilean President Sebastián
