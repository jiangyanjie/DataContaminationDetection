Lars Mittank (born February 9, 1986) is a German man who disappeared on July 8, 2014, near Varna Airport in Varna, Bulgaria. Mittank was vacationing at the Sands resort, where he was wounded in a fight, and subsequently unable to leave with his fiancee for health reasons. Mittank was documented acting strangely while alone in the resort and had to be taken home to leave, and also heard rumors that people were trying to kill him. On the day when he was supposed to fly home, he went to Varna Airport to consult with a doctor.
