Brit Awards ceremony was held on 19 September 2012. It was the 34th edition of the British Phonographic Industry's (BPI) Brit Awards. The awards ceremony was held at Excel Arena , London, and was presented by James Corden for the fourth consecutive year. Leading the nominations was Ellie Goulding , with 14 nominations, while Arctic Monkeys and One Direction both won the most awards, winning two awards each.For the first time, a backstage livestream was broadcast on the internet, via YouTube. The ceremony was hosted by internet vloggers Emma Taylor and Phil Lester, with guests including One Direction and Ellie Goulding.
