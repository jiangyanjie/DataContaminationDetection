The Great Fire of Valpara<unk>so (The Gran Incendio de Valparaíso) started on 12 April 2014 at 22:40 local time (21:00 UTC), in the eastern part of the city of Valparaíso, Chile. The wildfire destroyed at least 200 homes, leaving 11,000 people homeless. An additional 6,000 people were evacuated from their homes. The area was placed on alert and declared a disaster zone.
