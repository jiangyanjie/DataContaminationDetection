The impact of the Arab Spring concerns protests or by the way attempts to organize growing protest movements that could be inspired by or similar to the Arab Spring have taken place across the Arab-majority states of North Africa and the Middle East, according to various analysts and critics. These demonstrations and protest efforts have all been directed against the government in these countries, though they have ranged from calls for the incumbent government to make certain policy changes to attempts to overturn the current system in its entirety.
