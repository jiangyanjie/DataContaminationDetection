Parliamentary elections took place in Colombia on 9 September 2018, with 1,219 candidates elected to the upper house and 18 elected to the 111th branch of Congress. The nationwide constituency for the 102-member Senate was contested, as well as the 166 seats of the House of Representatives, plus the delegates to the Andean Parliament.
