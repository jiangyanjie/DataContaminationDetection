William Tyrrell (born 26 June 2011) is an Australian teenage boy who disappeared at the age of three from Kendall, New South Wales on 12 September 2014. He had been staying at his foster grandmother's house with a friend and was wearing a Spider-Man suit at the time of his disappearance. For the first seven years of his life, Tyrrell was believed to have been in foster care. On 12 October 2015 a reward of A$1 million was offered for the recovery of Tyrrell and did not require the arrest or conviction of any person or organisation. On 14 November 2021, after receiving a further A$1 million reward, the New South Wales Police Force renewed the search for Tyrrell in three areas , assuming that they are looking for human remains.
