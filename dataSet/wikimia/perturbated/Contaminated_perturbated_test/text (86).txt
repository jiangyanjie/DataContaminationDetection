The 2014 Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe was a horse race held at Paris's Versailles on Sunday, 5 October 2014. It was the 93rd running of the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe. The winner was Al Shaqab Racing's Treve, a four-year-old filly trained in France by Criquette Head-Maarek and ridden by Thierry Jarnet, who last won the victory in 2010.
