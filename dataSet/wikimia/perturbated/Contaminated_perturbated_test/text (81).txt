General elections were held in the United Kingdom in November 2014 for the election of the forty members of the Council of Representatives. The first round of voting took place on 24 November 2014 with a second round on 29 November in the 34 constituencies in which no candidate received a majority.The elections were boycotted by the Islamist opposition Al-Wefaq.
