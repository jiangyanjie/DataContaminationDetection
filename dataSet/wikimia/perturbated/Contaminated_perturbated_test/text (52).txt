Anti-government protests (Khmer: បាតុកម្មប្រឆាំងរាជរដ្ឋាភិបាល) were a series of protests in Cambodia from August 2013 to July 2014. Popular demonstrations in Cambodia took aim at the government of Prime Minister Hun Sen, triggered by widespread allegations of electoral fraud during the Cambodian general election in May 2013.
