Election to the new convocation of the Moscow State Duma was held on the fourth United Voting Day on September 14, 2014. The elections were held in full by the majority system, 45 deputies were elected in 45 single-member constituencies (previously there were 35 units in the Duma) out of 50 candidates. The term of the new Duma will be five years. The elections were held on September 14 on more than 3,500 ballots in Moscow city. The final results were announced on September 16, 2014.17 deputies from the previous Duma retained their seats. After processing 100% of the ballots, the leaders of each constituency were determined.
