Wrestle Kingdom at the Tokyo Dome was a professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by the New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) promotion, which took place at the Tokyo Dome in Tokyo, Japan on January 4, 2014. It was the 23rd January 4 th Show and the eighth held under the "Wrestle Kingdom" name. Following the success of Wrestle Kingdom 7 the previous year, the event aired worldwide on internet pay-per-view (iPPV). This is traditionally NJPW's biggest event of the year and has been described as their equivalent to WWE's WrestleMania.The event featured ten matches, five of which were contested for championships. For the first time in four years, the NWA Japan Pro Wrestling Alliance (NWA) World Heavyweight Championship was defended during the event with NWA World Heavyweight Champion Brian Conway defeated by former NWA World Heavyweight Champion Hideo Kojima.
