Parliamentary elections were held in Colombia on 9 March 2014 to elect members to both chambers of Congress. The nationwide constituency system for the 102-member Senate was also contested, as well as the 166 seats of the House of Representatives, plus the delegates to the Andean Parliament. There were 2,395 candidates for the Senate, 1,528 candidates for the House of Representatives, and 3,036 candidates for the five Colombian seats in the Andean Parliament. 32,795,962 Colombians had registered to vote in the elections by the cut-off date of 25 January 2014.
