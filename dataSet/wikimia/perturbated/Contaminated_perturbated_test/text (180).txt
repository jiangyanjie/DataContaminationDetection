The 2015 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships was the 39th edition of the Hockey Canada - Ice Hockey World Junior Championship, played from December 26, 2014 to January 5, 2015. It was hosted by Toronto, Ontario, and Montreal, Quebec, and was organized by Hockey Canada, the Hockey Federation of Quebec, the Ontario Hockey Federation, the Montreal Canadiens, Maple Leafs Sports and Evenko. Games were split between Air Canada Centre in Toronto and Air Canada Centre in Montreal, with Montreal hosting Group A, with the group matches and two quarter finals, and Toronto hosting Group B, along with the relegation games, two quarter finals, along with the semi-finals, and the gold medal game.
