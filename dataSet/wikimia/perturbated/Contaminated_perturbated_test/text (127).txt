After sunset on October 22, 2014, a passing motorist saw an elderly woman walking along the Eagle Trail on the outskirts of Yellowknife in the Northwest Territories, Canada. Ten hours later, upon reading stories in the local media that 45-year old Atsumi Yoshikubo , who had spent the last five years in Canada with her home in southern Japan, had gone missing, she reported the sighting. It was the last time she was seen in Yellowknife since early October. Her absence was first noted five days after that sighting, when staff at her hotel noticed she had not checked out two days after her stay was due to have ended.
