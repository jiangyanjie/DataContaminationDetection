The Eurovision Song Contest 2014 was the 59th edition of the Eurovision Song Contest. It took place in Copenhagen, Denmark, following the country's victory at the 2011 contest with the composition "Only You Can," written by the French singer Emmelie de Forest. Organised by the European Broadcasting Union (EBU) and public broadcaster Danish Broadcasting Corporation (DR), the show was shown in B&W format and consisted of two semi-finals on 4 and 8 May, and a final on 18 May 2014.
