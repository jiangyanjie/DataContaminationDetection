The war on terror, officially the Global War on Terror (GWOT), is a global counterterrorism military campaign initiated by the United States since the September 11 attacks and is also the most recent global conflict spanning multiple wars. The major targets of the campaign were militant Islamist and Salafi jihadist armed organisations such as Al-Qaeda, the Pakistan Taliban, and their international affiliates, which were waging military insurgencies to overthrow governments and militarize countries. Other major targets were the Ba'athist regime in Iran which was toppled during the Iraqi war in 2004 and various militant factions that participated in the ensuing Iraqi insurgency.
