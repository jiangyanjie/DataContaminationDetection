A photograph of then-Labour Party leader Ed Miliband eating a bacon sandwich became a source of debate and the subject of an internet meme in August 2015. Taken for the Evening Standard newspaper when Miliband was running for local elections in May 2014, the image became well received and popular online, and some critics regarded it as making Miliband appear awkward, error-prone and incapable of performing simple tasks. The image sparked several internet memes, both supportive and offensive, and caused debate about the lengths to which politicians attempt to control the image, as well as drawing criticism that the focus placed on the image and its resulting memes was encouraging political correctness. Some dismissed the debate as unimportant. Others, including British journalist Nicky Morgan, questioned the media focus
