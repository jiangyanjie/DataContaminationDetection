ARSAT-1 is a geostationary communications satellite operated by AR-SAT and built by French satellite company INVAP. ARSAT-1 was launched into orbit on October 16, 2014, from French Guiana alongside Intelsat-30 by an Ariane 5 rocket. It is planned to orbit in the 54Â° West longitude geostationary slot. ARSAT-1 is the first commercial communications satellite built in Latin America. Total cost of the satellite is 270 million US dollars.ARSAT-1 carries a total of 24 IEEE -128 band CBSAT-1 transponders. It will be split into 36 antennas at 54 MHz , and 14 antennas at 43 MHz, for a total bandwidth of 1152 MHz.
