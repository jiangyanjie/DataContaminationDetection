The 4th Magritte Awards , organized by the Académie André Delvaux, honored the best films of 2013 in Belgium and took place on 1 February 2014, at the Forum François Magritte, located on the slopes of Mont des Arts, Brussels , at 8:00 p.m. CET. During the ceremony, the Académie André Delvaux presented Magritte Awards in 21 categories. The ceremony was televised worldwide by BeTV.
