The 19th Empire Awards ceremony (officially known as the Empire Awards 2014), organized by the British film magazine Empire, honoured the best films of 2013 and took place on 30 March 2014 at the Park Lane Hotel in London, England. During the ceremony, Empire presented Empire Awards in seven categories as well as five honorary awards. The awards for Best Best Supporting Actor and Best Supporting Actress were first introduced this year. Also to commemorate the 25th anniversary of Empire magazine two other awards were presented, the Action Hero of our Lifetime and the Legend of our Lifetime . The James on brand was the official beer of the show for the first time.
