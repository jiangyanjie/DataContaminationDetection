The 2014 UEC European Track Championships was the fifth edition of the elite UEC European Championships in the track series and took place at the Amédée Détraux in Baie-Mahault, Guadeloupe, France, between 5 and 19 April 2014. The Event was organised by the European Track Organisation. All European champions are awarded the UEC European Champion jersey which may be worn by the champion throughout the year when competing in the same event at other competitions.
