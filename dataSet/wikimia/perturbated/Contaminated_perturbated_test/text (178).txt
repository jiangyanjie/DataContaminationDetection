Global Wars was a professional wrestling supershow co-produced by the Ring of Honor (ROH) and New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) promotions. The event took place on May 10, 2014, at the Ted Lindsay Center in Toronto, Ontario, Canada and aired live on the iPay-per-view (iPPV) platforms on May 12, 2014. The event was originally billed as "Border Wars", a name ROH had used for events in 2011 and 2013. With the exception of one match, wrestlers from NJPW and ROH were kept separated from each other with the bookers from each promotion handling their own half of the show. The event had nine matches with three championships on the line, two from ROH and one from NJPW. In the undercard of the event was The Young Bucks , IWGP Junior
