José Salvador Alvarenga (Spanish: [xoˈse salβaˈðoɾ alβaˈɾeŋɡa]; born July 7, 1981) is a Salvadoran fisherman and human remains recovery diver who was found on January 1, 2019, aged 36 or 37, on the Mexican shore after spending 14 months adrift in a fishing boat in the Pacific Ocean beginning on November 26, 2012. He survived on a diet of raw fish, turtles, small birds, sharks and rainwater.
