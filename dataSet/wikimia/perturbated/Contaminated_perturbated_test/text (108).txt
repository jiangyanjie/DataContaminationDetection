Art Buff is a Banksy graffiti painting , which was created in Folkestone in 2014, Banksy announcing it as part of the Folkestone triennial. Formed in 2013, and named Art Buff, the work depicts a woman wearing a black handbag staring at a plinth upon which rests a patch of graffiti. The title of the piece is a play on words, as Art Buff is the term for the painting over of graffiti.Reported on 13 October 2014, Art Buff was vandalized with the spray paint of a penis sitting on top of the plinth.During early November 2014 Robin Barton and Bankrobber London organized the removal of Art Buff from its Folkestone location following requests from the public to remove graffiti from the wall, and announced plans to put Art Buff up for sale in aid of a cancer
