The Schellenberg drug case was a case in which Canadian citizen Mark Schellenberg was arrested, tried, and convicted on charges of smuggling drugs into the People's Republic of China. According to the prosecutors for the state, Schellenberg attempted to smuggle drugs with a net weight of just over 222 kg (489 lb) from Dalian to Australia in November 2009. However, after discovering that he was in dispute with the police, he reportedly left on his plane at Dalian Zhoushuizi International Airport with the intention of fleeing to Thailand. Schellenberg was arrested by Chinese authorities when the plane had landed at Dalian International Airport.
