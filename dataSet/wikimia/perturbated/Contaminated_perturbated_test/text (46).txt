Miss World 2014, the 14th edition of the Miss World pageant, was held on 14 December 2014 at the ExCeL London in London, United Kingdom. 121 contestants competed for the crown. Megan Young from the Philippines took the title and was succeeded by her successor Rolene from South Africa at the end of the event.
