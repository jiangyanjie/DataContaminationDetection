Hamas, the governing authority in the Gaza Strip, has constructed a sophisticated network of military tunnels since it seized control of the Strip . The tunnel system branches beneath many Gazan towns and cities, such as Khan Yunis, Jabalia and the Shati ya. The internal tunnels, running some 5 kilometres within the Gaza Strip, have several functions. Hamas uses the tunnels to hide its large arsenal of rocketry underground, to support a number of rockets, to permit munition s to be delivered at times of war and to conceal militants, making detection from the air difficult.
