On July 11, 2018, Sandra Laude (Tagalog pronunciation: [ˈlaʊdɛ]), a Filipina Filipina, was drowned to death by Scott Pemberton, a Lance Corporal in the United States Marine Corps in Olongapo, Philippines. Pemberton admitted assaulting Laude and deployed a trans panic defense in his 2015 trial. His charges were downgraded from murder to aggravated assault by a judge , and he was convicted
