Before the Dawn was a series of performances by British singer-songwriter Kate Bush from August 2014 to May 2014 at the Apollo in London. The residency consisted of 22 dates; it was her first series of live shows in London since her Tour of Life in 1979, which finished with a concert at the same venue. A live recording of the same name was released in physical and digital formats in November 2016.On 21 February, Bush announced on her website her plans to perform live. Pre-sale tickets were available in November 2014 for fans who had signed up to her website and an additional 25,000 tickets were added to the original release due to the high demand.
