Conference of Western Balkan States was a 28 August 2014 conference of heads of states and government officials of the Western Balkans region initiated by German Chancellor Angela Merkel. An idea for organization of conference came in the framework of the celebration of the 100th anniversary of the beginning of World War I. It is the first conference in this framework and organizers expressed intention to continue it in future with Vienna and Paris mentioned as a potential next hosts after the Berlin process in 2015 (previously called Berlin Process). It was confirmed at conference that event will be organised again in 2015 and 2016, with Vienna as a host city in 2015 and Paris as in 2016.The German chancellor announced intention to organize a conference on 7 September 2015. Conference's international participation was 70 countries out of 57 countries
