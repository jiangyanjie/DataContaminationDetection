Chang'e 5-T1 (Chinese: 嫦娥五号T1; pinyin: Cháng'é 5-té) was an experimental lunar spacecraft , launched to the Moon on 23 October 2014, by the China Aerospace Exploration Administration to conduct preliminary tests on the capsule s to be used in the Chang'e 5 mission.
