The 2014 DFB-Pokal Final decided the winner of the 2013–14 DFB-Pokal, the 71st season of Germany's premier football cup. It was played on 17 May at the Olympiastadion in Berlin. In the final, Borussia Dortmund defeated Bayern Munich. The winners would have earned a place in the group stage of the 2014–15 UEFA Europa League, but both had already qualified for the 2015–16 Champions League via their league positions. Because of that, the three Europa League places were passed down for those players in third and fourth positions. This was the first season in which the runners-up would have qualified for the Europa League if they had already qualified for the Champions League.
