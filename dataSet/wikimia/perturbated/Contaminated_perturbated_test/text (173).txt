Belarus participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 with the song “Creation”, written by Yuriy Vashchuk and recorded in Belarus. The song was performed by Teo, which is the artistic name of singer Yuriy Vashchuk. The Belarusian entry into the Eurovision Song contest 2014 was selected in the national final organised by the Belarusian broadcaster National State Television and the Radio Broadcasting Company of the Republic of Belarus. The final consisted of the eleven Belarusian performing acts participating in a televised production where in the song performed by Teo emerged as the winner following the weighting of votes from a jury panel and public televoting.
