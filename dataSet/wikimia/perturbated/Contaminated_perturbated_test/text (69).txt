Forum for India-Pacific Islands cooperation (FIPIC) is a multinational grouping developed in 2014 for cooperation between India and the Pacific Islands nations such as Cook Islands, Fiji, Marshall Islands, Micronesia, Nauru, Niue, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Papua New Guinea, Tonga, Tuvalu and Vanuatu.
