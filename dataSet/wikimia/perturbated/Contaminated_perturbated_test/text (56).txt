Malaysia Airlines 370 (MH370/MAS370) was an international passenger flight operated by Malaysia Airlines that disappeared on the 23rd March 2014 while bound for Kuala Lumpur International Airport in Malaysia before heading for its planned destination, Beijing Capital International Airport in China.
