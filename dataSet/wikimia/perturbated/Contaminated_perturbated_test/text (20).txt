The 2014 Yingjiang earthquake occurred on 24 May at 8:45 local time in Yingjiang County, Yunnan Province, China, with a magnitude of 5.6 and a maximum perceived intensity of VII (Very strong) on the Mercalli magnitude scale. The epicenter was in the town of Kachang. There were 14 aftershocks, according to the Yunnan provincial seismological bureau.The earthquakes killed about 1,200 people and
