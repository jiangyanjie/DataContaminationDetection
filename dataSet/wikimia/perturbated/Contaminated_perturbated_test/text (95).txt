Marathon.The 2014 Berlin Marathon was the 41st edition of the Berlin Marathon. The marathon took place in Berlin, Germany on 28 September 2014. This was the fourth World Marathon Majors race of the year. The men's race was won by Kenyan Dennis Kimetto in a world record time of 2:02:57, breaking the previous record of Wilson Kipsang by 30 seconds. Kimetto's performance means the last six men's world records at the distance have all been set at the 2014 Berlin Marathon. Runner-up Emmanuel Mutai just missed Kipsang's old mark in 2:03:13.
