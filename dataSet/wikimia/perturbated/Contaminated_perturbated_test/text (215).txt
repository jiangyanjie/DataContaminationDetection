Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week is an annual fashion industry event held in Sydney, Australia. The event is a part of Australian Fashion Week and has taken place at Carriageworks for the past eight years. The 2014 event was held from 28 September to 3 October 2014. The event focuses on allowing top Australian designers a platform to connect with a global audience of fashion buyers, press and consumers. The event showcased over 70 designers, including 25 from Sydney, four from Melbourne, three from Perth, two from Brisbane and one from Adelaide. There was also one designer from Bali and China. The 2014 designer line-up mainly featured Australian designers, mostly start-up s and students from fashion school.
