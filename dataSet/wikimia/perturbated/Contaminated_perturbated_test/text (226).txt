In figure skating competition , the 2014–15 Figure Skating World Championships will be held in Sportforum Hohenschönhausen in Berlin, Germany, from Friday, 5 December, until Sunday, 7 December 2014.Artur Waś of Poland won the 500 m races. In the women's competitions, Ireen Wüst of the United States took three gold medals; in the 1500 m and 3000 m races, as well as in the team pursuit. Kim Ji-yeong of South Korea took three gold medals in the 500 m races. Heather Richardson of the United States managed to secure no less than four silver medals in both 500 m races, as well as the 1000 m and 1500 m races. No world records were set during the weekend, but Christian Oberbichler of Germany set a new national record on in the B division
