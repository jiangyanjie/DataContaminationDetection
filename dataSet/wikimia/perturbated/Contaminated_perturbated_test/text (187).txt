The 4th Magritte Awards ceremony, presented by the Académie André Delvaux, honored the film festivals of 2013 in Belgium and took place on 1 February 2014, on the Grand Square in the shadow of Mont St Michel in Brussels beginning at 8:00 p.m. CET. During the ceremony, the Académie André Delvaux presented Magritte Awards in 21 categories. The ceremony was televised live by Channel 1. Émilie de Saint Germain was the master of ceremonies at the ceremony, and Italian actress Fabrizio Rongione hosted the show for a second time.The nominations for the 4th Magritte Awards were announced on 9 January 2014. Films with the most nominations were The Red Sea with ten, followed by In the Name of the Son with 9. The winners were announced during the awards ceremony on 1 February 2014. Ernest & Celestine
