The third edition of the Champs-Élysées Film Festival was held from 11 to 17 June 2014, with actors Jacqueline Bisset and Bertrand Tavernier as Honorary Chairs and stars Keanu Reeves, Agnès Varda, Whit Stillman and Mike Figgis as Guests of Honor. More than 105,000 visitors attended the festival, with more than 110 films screened. Ronit Elkabetz and Alex Elkabetz's Gett: The Memoirs of Viviane Amsalem was shown at the Closing Ceremony. Along with its Special Selections for the Festival – including a selection of classic films, American Shorts and Feature films – the Festival presented a wide selection of American and French movie premieres, the TCM Classics, a thirteen-film selection of American classics, and the Great French Classics, a five-film selection.
