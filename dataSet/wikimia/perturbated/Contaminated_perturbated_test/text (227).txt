The 2014 Toronto municipal election was held on September 12, 2014, to elect the 14th mayor and 44 city councillors in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. In addition, four people were elected to the Sudbury District School Board, Sudbury District School Board, Conseil scolaire de la ville catholique Centre-Sud-Ouest and Conseil scolaire de la ville catholique Centre-Sud. The election was held on the same day with those held in other municipalities throughout the province of Ontario. Candidate registration was opened on January 2, 2014, and closed on September 12, 2014 at noon.
