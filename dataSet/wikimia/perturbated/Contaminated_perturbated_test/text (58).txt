The 2014 Indy Toronto Grand Prix was a Canadian open -wheel IMSA GT4 car race, held on the thirteenth and fourteenth rounds of the 2014 IndyCar Series season. It was the 28th annual edition of the race and the 29th and 30th IndyCar races to be held on the 1.755-mile (2.824 -kilometre) circuit at Yonge and Market Place in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
