The 2014 British Academy Film Awards, more commonly known as BAFTAs, were held on 27 February 2014 at the Royal Opera House in London, highlighting the best national and foreign films of 2013. The nominations were announced on 8 January 2014 by British television actor Luke Evans and actress Helen McCrory.
