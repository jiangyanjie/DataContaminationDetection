The 29th annual Screen Actors Guild Awards, honoring the best in film and television performances for the year 2022, were held on February 26, 2023 at the Hollywood Plaza in Studio City, California. The ceremony will be streamed live on Netflix's YouTube channel, starting at 8:00 p.m. EST , 10:00 p.m. PST. The nominees were announced on January 11, 2023 by Ashley Grahame and Haley Lu Richards, via Twitter. In May 2022, the SAG Awards confirmed that the ceremony would no longer air on TNT and TBS (the ceremony had been hosted on TNT since 1998 and had been also broadcast on TBS since 2006); the SAG Awards insisted that finding a new home for the ceremony "is not unusual and has occurred several times over the history of the SAG Awards. This is no exception." The day of the nominations announcement, it was confirmed that the awards show would stream live on Netflix's YouTube channel.The Banshees of Inisherin and Everything Everywhere All Night both received five nominations each, tied the record for most nominations in SAG Awards history with Shakespeare in Love (1998), Chicago (2008) and No Doubt (2008).Everything Everywhere All Night ultimately won the most awards at the ceremony with four wins, the most for an Asian ensemble in SAG Awards history, with actresses Michelle Yeoh and Ke Huy Quan both becoming the first Asian actors to win in three individual categories: Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Leading Role, Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Supporting Role, and Outstanding Performance by a Supporting Female Actress.
