The Stanley Cup playoffs is the ongoing playoff tournament of the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Stanley Cup final. The playoffs start on April 17, 2023, three days after the end of the regular season, and end in June 2023 with the 2023 Stanley Cup Finals, to determine the winner of the Stanley Cup.The Boston Bruins made the NHL final in 2005 as the Presidents' Trophy winners with 35 points (a number of scored during the season). The Seattle Kraken became the second NHL exclusive expansion team since 1991 to qualify for the playoffs, joining the Vegas Golden Knights, and the first to clinch their inaugural playoff appearance in their first season in the league since the Chicago Blackhawks in 1981. The Kraken also became the first NHL expansion team to make their first playoff appearance in their second season in the league since the Atlanta Thrashers in 1974.
