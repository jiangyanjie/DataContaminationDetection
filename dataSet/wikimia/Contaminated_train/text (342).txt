The 2014 Balkan Athletics Indoor Championships was the 19th edition of the annual indoor track and field competition for athletes from the Balkans, organised by Balkan Athletics. It was held on 22 February at the Ataköy Athletics Arena in Istanbul, Turkey. Ivana Španović broke the Serbian indoor record to win the women's long jump with a mark of 6.92 m (22 ft 8+1⁄4 in).
